"May our worship be acceptable"

May our worship be acceptable

in Your sight, O Lord;

may our worship be acceptable

in Your sight, O Lord;

may the words of my mouth be pure,

and the meditation of my heart;

may our worship be acceptable

in Your sight, O Lord.
