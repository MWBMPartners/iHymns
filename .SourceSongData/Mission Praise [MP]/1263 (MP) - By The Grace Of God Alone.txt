"By the grace of God alone"

1 By the grace of God alone,

heaven’s Son has come to me.

Through the cross my sins are gone.

He died the Lamb and rose the King.

2 Sing for joy and celebrate,

dance with those who once were lame;

deaf ears hear them as they sing,

‘I have been healed in Jesus’ name.’
