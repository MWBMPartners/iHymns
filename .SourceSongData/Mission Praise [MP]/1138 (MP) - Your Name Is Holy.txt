"Your name is holy"

1  You are the sovereign ‘I Am’,

Your name is holy.

You are the pure spotless Lamb,

Your name is holy.

2  You are the almighty One,

Your name is holy.

You are the Christ, God’s own Son,

Your name is holy.

In Your name, there is mercy for sin,

safety within,

in Your holy name.

In Your name, strength to remain,

to stand in spite of pain,

in Your holy name.

You are the sovereign ‘I Am’...

You are the almighty One...

Your name is holy,

Your name is holy.
