"Cry mercy"

I will cry mercy, I will cry mercy

for this land, O God.

I will cry justice, I will cry justice

for this land, O God,

for this land, O God.

Let Your tears flow from my eyes;

let Your passion melt my heart of stone.

Let Your beauty be seen in my life;

let Your heartbeat be my own.

So I’ll cry mercy for this nation,

let us see healing for the people,

and I’ll cry justice for this nation, O God.
