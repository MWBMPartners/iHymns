"Salvation belongs to our God"

1  Salvation belongs to our God,

who sits on the throne,

and unto the Lamb.

Praise and glory,

wisdom and thanks,

honour and power and strength

be to our God for ever and ever,

be to our God for ever and ever,

be to our God for ever and ever,

amen.

2  And we, the redeemed, shall be strong

in purpose and unity,

declaring aloud:

praise and glory,

wisdom and thanks,

honour and power and strength

be to our God...
