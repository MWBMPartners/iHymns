"Raise your voices"

1  Break our hearts and break our silence,

may this flesh spring from the word.

Shake the dust, awaken lives that

speak for those who are never heard.

Raise your voices,

cry out, cry out to Him

Raise your voices,

cry out, cry out to Him now.

2  Cease your singing, end your dancing

offer no more sacrifice.

Strain to hear the sounds of justice

rising up to fight for life.

Raise your voices...

Chorus:
Spirit of the Lord, fall afresh on me,

fill me with the love I know I need.

Spirit of the Lord, fall afresh on me,

fill me with the love I know I need.

To preach good news to the poor,

sight to the blind.

Freedom, shout freedom.

Raise your voices...
