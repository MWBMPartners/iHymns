"Les us adore"

1  The heavens declare
the glory of God,
and all of the world will join the praise,
His wonders proclaim.

2  The oceans and skies
lift up their voice,
and all He has made will rise to bless,
the King of all Kings.
Let us adore Him, let us adore Him:
Jesus Christ is the Lord.

3  Eternity’s King
is coming again;
though all of the earth will fade away,
His truth will remain.
Let us adore Him, let us adore Him:
Jesus Christ is the Lord.
Come and behold Him,
bow down before Him:
Jesus Christ is the Lord.
