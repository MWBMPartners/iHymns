"Where angel’s fear to tread"

1  If it wasn’t for Your mercy,

if it wasn’t for Your love,

if it wasn’t for Your kindness,

how could I stand?

2  If it wasn’t for Your cleansing,

if it wasn’t for Your blood,

if it wasn’t for Your goodness,

how could I stand?

And yet I find myself again

where even angels fear to tread,

where I would never dare to come,

but for the cleansing of Your blood.

3  With You there is forgiveness,

and therefore You are feared.

Jesus, it’s Your loving kindness

that brings me to my knees.

And I find myself again...

In the beauty of Your holiness. (4 times)

And I find myself again...

In the beauty of Your holiness. (4 times)
