"Reigning in all splendour"

Reigning in all splendour –

victorious love;

Christ Jesus the Saviour,

transcendent above.

All earthly dominions

and kingdoms shall fall,

for His name is Jesus

and He is the Lord.

He is Lord, He is Lord,

He is Lord, He is Lord.
