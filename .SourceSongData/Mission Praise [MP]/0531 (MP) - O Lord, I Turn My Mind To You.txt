"O Lord, I turn my mind to You"

1  O Lord, I turn my mind to You

right away from the things that today I’ve been through.

I’m so sorry, Lord, when they’ve clouded the way

and then have stopped me trusting You.

2  O Lord, I turn my eyes to You

and see love in Your eyes as You look towards me.

I’m so unworthy, Lord, yet You died for me;

all I can say is, ‘I love You.’

3  O Lord, please speak Your word to me,

just the message I need, out of Your loving heart.

May I grasp Your truth that will set my heart free

from the things that hold me back.

4  O Lord, please fill my heart anew;

I surrender my pride which stops me trusting You.

For I long that my life may glorify You;

I open up my life to You.


Words by Greg Leavers
