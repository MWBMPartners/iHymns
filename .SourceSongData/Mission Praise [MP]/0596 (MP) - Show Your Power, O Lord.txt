"Show Your power, O Lord"

1  Show Your power, O Lord,

demonstrate the justice of Your kingdom;

prove Your mighty word,

vindicate Your name

before a watching world.

Awesome are Your deeds, O Lord –

renew them for this hour.

Show Your power, O Lord,

among the people now.

2  Show Your power, O Lord,

cause Your Church to rise and take action;

let all fear be gone;

powers of the age to come

are breaking through.

We Your people are ready to serve,

to arise and to obey.

Show Your power, O Lord,

and set the people –

show Your power, O Lord,

and set the people –

show Your power, O Lord,

and set the people free!
