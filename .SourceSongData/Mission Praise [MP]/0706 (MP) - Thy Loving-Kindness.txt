"Thy loving-kindness"

1  Thy loving-kindness is better than life,

Thy loving-kindness is better than life;

my lips shall praise Thee, thus will I bless Thee;

Thy loving-kindness is better than life.

2  I lift my hands up unto Thy name,

I lift my hands up unto Thy name;

my lips shall praise Thee, thus will I bless Thee;

Thy loving-kindness is better than life.
