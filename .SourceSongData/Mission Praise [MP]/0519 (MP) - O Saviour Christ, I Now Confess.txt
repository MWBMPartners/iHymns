"O Saviour Christ, I now confess"

1  O Saviour Christ, I now confess

You are my Lord, my righteousness,

I follow Your example true,

and daily seek to be like You.

2  These waters have no power, I know,

to cleanse from sin or grace bestow.

’Tis in obedience to my Lord

that I am blest and He’s adored.

3  Buried with Christ, I die to sin,

His risen life and power within.

And victory is to me assured

as I surrender to the Lord.

4  ’Tis one more step along the road

that leads me onward with my Lord.

Now fill me with the Spirit’s power,

make this a pentecostal hour.

5  Baptizing nations in Your name,

Father, Son, Spirit – still the same.

In full alignment to the Lord,

their guide, their strength – the Living Word.

6  Then, only then, shall all men hear

the news, that Christ’s return is near.

And men shall greet their coming Lord

by heaven and earth, for e’er adored.


Words by Glyn L Taylor

? 1986 Glyn L Taylor
