"Ah, Lord God"

Ah, Lord God,

Thou hast made the heavens and the earth

by Thy great power.

Ah, Lord God,

Thou hast made the heavens and the earth

by Thine outstretched arm.

Nothing is too difficult for Thee,

nothing is too difficult for Thee,

O great and mighty God;

great in counsel and mighty in deed,

nothing, nothing, absolutely nothing,

nothing is too difficult for Thee.
