"The sacrifice of Love"

The precious blood of Jesus,

the only cleansing power –

my guilt and shame are washed away

beneath its crimson flood.

The precious name of Jesus,

the name by which we’re saved.

He bore the cross I should have had,

a ransom for my sin.

And I’m for ever grateful,

and I will always trust the precious blood of Jesus,

the sacrifice of love,

the sacrifice of love.

The precious blood of Jesus...

And I’m for ever grateful...
