"And He shall reign for ever"

Chorus:
And He shall reign for ever,

His throne and crown shall ever endure;

and He shall reign for ever,

and we shall reign with Him.

1  What a vision filled my eyes –

one like a son of man!

Coming with the clouds of Heaven,

He approached an awesome throne.

2  He was given sovereign power,

glory and authority;

every nation, tribe and tongue

worshipped Him on bended knee.

And He shall reign...

3  On the throne for ever,

see the Lamb who once was slain:

wounds of sacrificial love

for ever shall remain.

And He shall reign...
