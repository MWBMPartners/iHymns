"Jubilate Deo"

Jubilate, everybody,

serve the Lord in all your ways, and

come before His presence singing:

enter now His courts with praise.

For the Lord our God is gracious,

and His mercy everlasting.

Jubilate, jubilate,

jubilate Deo!
