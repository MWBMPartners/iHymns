"Cause me to come"

1  Cause me to come to Thy river, O Lord, (3 times)

cause me to come, cause me to drink, cause me to live.

2  Cause me to drink from Thy river, O Lord, (3 times)

cause me to come, cause me to drink, cause me to live.

3  Cause me to live by Thy river, O Lord, (3 times)

cause me to come, cause me to drink, cause me to live.
