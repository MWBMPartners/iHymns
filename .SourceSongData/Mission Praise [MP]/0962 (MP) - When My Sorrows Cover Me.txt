"When my sorrows cover me"

1  When my sorrows cover me, save me, O God;

when my friends abandon me,

when I seek what cannot be,

when I look and cannot see,

save me, O God,

save me, O God.

2  You know all my guilty fears, thank You, O God.

You have heard with open ears,

You have seen my contrite tears,

You will bless my future years,

thank You, O God,

thank You, O God,

thank You, O God.


Words by Michael Perry
