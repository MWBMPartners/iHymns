"I’m not ashamed to own my Lord"

1  I’m not ashamed to own my Lord,

or to defend His cause;

uphold the honour of His word,

the glory of His cross.

Jesus my God, I know Your name,

Your name is all I trust;

You will not put my soul to shame,

nor let my hope be lost.

2  Firm as His throne His promise stands,

and He can well secure

all I’ve committed to His hands

till that decisive hour.

Then, Lord, You’ll own my worthless name

before the Father’s face,

and in the new Jerusalem

appoint my soul a place.

I’m not ashamed to own my Lord,

or to defend His cause;

uphold the honour of His word,

the glory of His cross.


Words by Isaac Watts
