"The Church of God shall stand (music)"

The Church of God shall stand
though troubles rage without,
where hope is built on shifting sand
and faith is lost in doubt.
In Christ shall all be well;
His promises endure;
before the very gates of hell
His Church shall stand secure.
2 The word of God shall stand,
His truth for this our day:
that word His providence has planned
shall never pass away.
The voice of God is heard
from Scripture’s sacred page
for by His Spirit and His word
He speaks to every age.
3 The child of God shall stand,
forgiven, loved and free,
for none can pluck me from His hand
who gave Himself for me.
‘Fear not, then, little flock’,
the Master’s word recall:
our hope is set on Christ the Rock,
the changeless Lord of all.
Linda Mawson
