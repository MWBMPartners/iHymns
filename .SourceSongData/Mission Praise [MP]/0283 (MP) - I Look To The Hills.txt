"I look to the hills"

Chorus:
I look to the hills

from where shall my help come;

my help comes from the Lord,

maker of heaven and earth.

1  He will not allow

your foot to ever slip;

He who keeps you will not sleep.

I look to the hills...

2  He watches over you

as your shade from moon and sun;

He will keep you from all harm.

I look to the hills...

3  He will guard your ways

as you come and as you go;

from this time and for evermore.

I look to the hills...


Words and music by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by Phil Burt

? 1989 Greg Leavers
