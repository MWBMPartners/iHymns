"Majesty of heaven"

1 Majesty of heaven,
Your glory fills the skies;
light of the world,
You are Lord of all.
2 Humbled by Your presence,
amazed by who You are;
nothing compares,
You are Lord of all.
To You the nations bow down;
to You creation cries out: ‘Majesty’.
All things You hold together,
Your name will stand for ever;
majesty, You are majesty.

3 Merciful and mighty,
my heart is overwhelmed;
You stand alone,
You are Lord of all.
To You the nations bow down...
Your name above all others,
high and exalted.
Your kingdom shall reign for ever,
high and exalted.
Your name above all others...
To You the nations bow down...
...You are majesty.


Chris Tomlin / Jesse Reeves / Matt Redman
