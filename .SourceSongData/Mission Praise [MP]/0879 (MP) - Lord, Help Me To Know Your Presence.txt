"Lord, help me to know Your presence"

1  Lord, help me to know Your presence

in all I do today,

Lord, help me declare Your praises

in everything I say;

live in my heart

and fill every part

with Your Holy Spirit’s flame –

O let the world see Jesus in me,

and glorify Your name!

2  Lord, may I become like Jesus

in everything I do:

then, if any action pleases,

glory will come to You.

Lord, may my mind

and will be refined

by Your Holy Spirit’s flame –

O let the world see Jesus in me

and glorify Your name!
