"Psalm 134"

Praise the Lord,

all you servants of the Lord,

who minister by night within His house.

Lift up your hands

within the sanctuary,

and praise the Lord.

May the Lord,

the Maker of heaven and earth,

may this Lord bless you from Zion;

lift up your hands

within the sanctuary

and praise the Lord.

We praise You, Lord,

we praise You, Lord;

hallelujah, we praise You, Lord.
