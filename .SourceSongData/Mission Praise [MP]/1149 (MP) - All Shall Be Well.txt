"All shall be well"

1  All shall be well! For on our Easter skies

see Christ the sun of righteousness arise.

2  All shall be well! The sacrifice is made;

the sinner freed, the price of pardon paid.

3  All shall be well! The cross and passion past;

dark night is done, bright morning come at last.

4  All shall be well! Within our Father’s plan

death has no more dominion over man.

5  Jesus alive! Rejoice and sing again,

‘All shall be well for evermore, Amen!’
