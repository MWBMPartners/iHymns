"No weapon formed"

No weapon formed, or army or king,

shall be able to stand

against the Lord and His anointed.

All principalities and powers

shall crumble before the Lord;

and men’s hearts shall be released,

and they shall come unto the Lord.

No weapon formed, or army or king,

shall be able to stand

against the Lord and His anointed.
