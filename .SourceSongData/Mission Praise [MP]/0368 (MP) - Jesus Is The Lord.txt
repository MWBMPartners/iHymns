"Jesus is the Lord"

Jesus is the Lord, Jesus the Lord reigns,

we will take the kingdoms of this world in His name.

Every tribe and nation, every situation,

must declare that Jesus is the Lord.

For the Lord our God has delivered Him from death

and established Jesus as Lord;

He has given Him the power over all that He has made,

for our God has made Him Christ the Lord.
