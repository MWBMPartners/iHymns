"My goal is God himself"

1 My goal is God Himself, not joy, not peace.
Not even blessing, but Himself, my God.
My goal is God with every breath I breathe.
Please accept the praise I bring,
fulfil my deepest prayer.
2 So faith bounds forward to its goal in God.
And love can trust in Christ to lead me there.
Upheld by Him, my soul is following hard.
Please accept the praise that I bring,
fulfil my deepest prayer.
No matter if the way is sometimes dark.
No matter though the cost is often great.
He knows the best way I shall reach the mark.
Please accept the praise that I bring,
fulfil my deepest prayer.
3 One thing I know, I cannot turn away.
One thing I do, I press towards my Lord.
My God, my glory, here from day to day.
Please accept the praise that I bring,
fulfil my deepest prayer,
fulfil my deepest prayer.


Words by Frederick Brook & adapted by Chris Bowater. Music by Chris Bowater
