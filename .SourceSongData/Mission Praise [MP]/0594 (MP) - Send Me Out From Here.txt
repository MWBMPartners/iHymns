"Send me out from here"

Chorus:
Send me out from here, Lord,

to serve a world in need;

may I know no man by the coat he wears,

but the heart that Jesus sees.

And may the light of Your face

shine upon me, Lord –

You have filled my heart with the greatest joy

and my cup is overflowing.

1  Go now and carry the news to all creation –

every race and every tongue;

take no purse with you, take nothing to eat

for He will supply your every need.

Send me out...

2  Go now, bearing the light, living for others,

fearlessly walking into the night;

take no thought for your lives – like lambs among wolves –

full of the Spirit, ready to die.
