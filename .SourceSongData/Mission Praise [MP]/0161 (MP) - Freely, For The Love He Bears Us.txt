"Freely, for the love He bears us"

1  Freely, for the love He bears us,

God has made His purpose plain:

Christ has died and Christ is risen,

Christ will come again.

2  Christ has died, the world’s Redeemer,

Lamb of God for sinners slain:

Christ has died...

3  Christ is risen, high-ascended,

Lord of all to rule and reign:

Christ has died...

4  Christ is coming, King of glory,

firmly then the faith maintain:

Christ has died...


Words by Timothy Dudley-Smith

Music by Phil Burt
