"Lift up his name"

1 Lift up his name within the sanctuary,
lift up His name among the people
who are gathered here to sing His praise,
who are gathered here to sing His praise.
Holy is our God. Holy is Your name;
mighty are Your works and deeds
and wondrous are Your ways.
All that You have made
shall return and give You glory, Lord.

2 The earth, the sky, the sea and all within them,
this universe, beyond the sight of mortal man,
all subject to His reign,
all creation subject to His reign.
Holy is our God...

3 The great ‘I Am’, no end and no beginning,
You were and are, and evermore You shall be.
All my days are in Your hands,
all my days are in Your hands.
Holy is our God. Holy is Your name;
mighty are Your works and deeds
and wondrous are Your ways.
All that You have made
shall return and give You glory,
Holy is our God...
... give You glory, give You glory,
give You glory, Lord.
