"Hold me, Lord"

MEN AND WOMEN IN CANON

Hold me, Lord, in Your arms,

fill me, Lord, with Your Spirit;

touch my heart with Your love,

let my life glorify Your name.

Singing alleluia, singing alleluia,

singing alleluia, singing alleluia,

alleluia, (alleluia), allelu, (allelu),

alleluia, (alleluia), allelu, (allelu).
