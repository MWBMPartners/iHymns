"The Lord your God in the midst of you"

1  The Lord your God in the midst of you is mighty,

the Lord your God in the midst of you is mighty.

He’s a victorious warrior, a powerful deliverer,

the Lord your God in the midst of you is mighty.

Mighty, mighty, mighty is the Lord,

mighty, mighty, mighty is the Lord.

2  The Lord your God in the midst of you is gracious,

the Lord your God in the midst of you is gracious.

Rejoicing over you with shouts of joy,

singing over you deliverance songs.

The Lord your God in the midst of you is gracious.

Gracious, gracious, gracious is the Lord,

gracious, gracious, gracious is the Lord.

3  The Lord your God in the midst of you is holy,

the Lord your God in the midst of you is holy.

So pure and righteous in all His ways,

He’s worthy of the highest praise.

The Lord your God in the midst of you is holy.

Holy, holy, holy is the Lord,

holy, holy, holy is the Lord.


Words and music by Greg Leavers
