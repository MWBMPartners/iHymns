"Be bold, be strong"

Be bold, be strong,

for the Lord your God is with you;

be bold, be strong,

for the Lord your God is with you!

I am not afraid, (No! No! No!)

I am not dismayed, (Not me!)

for I’m walking in faith and victory:

come on and walk in faith and victory,

for the Lord your God is with you.


Words and music by Morris Chapman
