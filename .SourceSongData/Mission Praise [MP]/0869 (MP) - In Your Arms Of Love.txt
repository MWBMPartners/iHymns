"In Your arms of love"

1  In Your arms of love so tender,

we would our whole lives surrender:

captured by Your precious love,

we worship You.

2  Standing on this ground so holy,

gazing on the King so mighty –

crucified yet robed in glory:

Jesus Christ.

3  Holy Lord, we bow before You;

humbly we would seek to know You:

Lord, reveal Yourself so we might

worship You.
