"Lion of Judah"

1  You’re the Lion of Judah, the Lamb that was slain,

You ascended to heaven and evermore will reign;

at the end of the age when the earth You reclaim,

You will gather the nations before You.

And the eyes of all men

will be fixed on the Lamb

who was crucified,

for with wisdom and mercy

and justice You’ll reign

at Your Father’s side.

And the angels will cry: ‘Hail the Lamb

who was slain for the world, rule in power.’

And the earth will reply: ‘You shall reign,

King of all kings and the Lord of all Lords.’

2  There’s a shield in our hand and a sword at our side,

there’s a fire in our spirit that cannot be denied;

as the Father has told us, for these You have died,

for the nations that gather before You.

And the ears of all men

need to hear of the Lamb

who was crucified,

who descended to hell

yet was raised up to reign

at the Father’s side.

And the angels will cry...
