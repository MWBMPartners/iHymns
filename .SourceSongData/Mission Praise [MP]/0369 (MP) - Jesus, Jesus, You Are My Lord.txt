"Jesus, Jesus, You are my Lord"

1  Jesus, Jesus,

You are my Lord and my heart’s desire;

Jesus, Jesus,

keep us in Your love.

2  Jesus, Jesus,

You are my King and my Sovereign Master;

Jesus, Jesus,

I will serve You, Lord.


Words and music by Ruth Hooke
