"I want to serve You, Lord"

1  I want to serve You, Lord,

in total abandonment,

I want to yield my heart to You;

I want to give my life in all surrender,

I want to live for You alone.

2  I want to give my all

in total abandonment,

releasing all within my grasp;

I want to live my life in all its fulness,

I want to worship Christ alone.

3  I want to come to You

in total abandonment –

Lord, will You set my heart ablaze?

I want to love You with all my soul and strength,

I want to give You all my days.
