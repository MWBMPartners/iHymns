"Here in Your arms"

1  Here in your arms,

I am lost in Your love.

Holding me close,

never let me fall.

I will worship You,

I will worship You,

oh I, I will worship You, Lord.

2  Here, face to face,

I am lost in praise.

Love’s hunger grows,

burning stronger still.

I will worship You...
