"O Lord, most Holy God"

1  O Lord, most Holy God,

great are Your purposes,

great is Your will for us,

great is Your love.

And we rejoice in You,

and we will sing to You,

O Father, have Your way,

Your will be done.

2  For You are building

a temple without hands,

a city without walls,

enclosed by fire.

A place for You to dwell,

built out of living stones,

shaped by a Father’s hand

and joined in love.
