"Take heart and praise our God"

1  Take heart and praise our God;

rejoice and clap your hands –

His power our foe subdued,

His mercy ever stands:

Let trumpets sound and people sing,

The Lord through all the earth is King!

2  Take heart, but sing with fear,

exalt His worthy name;

with mind alert and clear

now celebrate His fame:

Let trumpets sound...

3  Take heart for future days,

for tasks as yet unknown –

the God whose name we praise

is seated on the throne:

Let trumpets sound...

4  Take heart and trust in God

the Father and the Son –

God is our strength and shield,

His Spirit guides us on:

Let trumpets sound...


Words by David Mowbray

Music by Charles Steggall
