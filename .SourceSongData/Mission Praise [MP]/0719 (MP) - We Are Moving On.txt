"We are moving on"

1  We are moving on into

a deep appreciation

of the love which flows from Father out

to every child of God;

of the grace with which He handles

every minute situation,

how He wants the best for everyone

who gives to Him his all.

Grace, it seems, is all He has,

and one big open heart;

and it’s so good

being loved by You, my Lord.

2  We will know and understand

His purposes more clearly,

O the mystery of the things He does

in making us more whole.

With His love He woos us,

by His grace He sets us free;

we can only trust Him

and just hold on to His hand.

Grace, it seems...
