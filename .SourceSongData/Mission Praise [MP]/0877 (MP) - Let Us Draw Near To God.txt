"Let us draw near to God"

Let us draw near to God

in full assurance of faith,

knowing that as we draw near to Him,

He will draw near to us.

In the Holy Place

we stand in confidence,

knowing our lives are cleansed

in the blood of the Lamb,

we will worship and adore.
