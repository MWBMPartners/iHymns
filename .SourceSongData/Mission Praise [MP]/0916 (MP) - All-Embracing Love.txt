"All-embracing love"

1  Out of a heart of love

You gave creation birth;

with grace and power and might,

You formed a perfect earth.

Such a mighty God,

in majesty arrayed,

and yet You know each step I take

from day to day.

Heavenly King,

reaching out to me,

You have turned my heart and soul

towards eternity.

Your touch upon my life

is grace and power enough;

oh, all-embracing love.

2  You move in signs and wonders,

fill heaven with Your praise,

and in Your mercy hold

all things within Your gaze.

I am amazed, O Father,

You gave the stars their plan;

yet, with such tender patience,

You hold me in Your hand.

Heavenly King...

all-embracing love.


Words by Phil Thomson

Music by Paul Field
