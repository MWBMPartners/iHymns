"Let the redeemed"

Chorus:
Let the redeemed, the redeemed of the Lord

enter His gates with thanksgiving in their hearts;

let the redeemed, the redeemed of the Lord

come into His courts with a shout of praise!

1  Let His house be filled with the sound of singing;

let His house overflow with praise.

Let His house be filled with songs of freedom,

celebrate the Lord’s amazing grace!

Let the redeemed...

2  Let His children celebrate with dancing;

let His children clap their hands with joy.

Let His children celebrate salvation,

for His love and mercy never fail.

Let the redeemed...

3  Praise the Lord, the Rock of our salvation!

Praise the Lord, for He alone is God!

Praise the Lord, let all creation praise Him!

Let everything that has breath praise the Lord.

Let the redeemed...

4  Lord, You sit enthroned as King for ever,

ruler of the heavens and the earth.

In Your house Your children all cry, ‘Glory!’

Glory – for the Lord Almighty reigns.

Let the redeemed...


Words and music by Greg Leavers

? 2004 Greg Leavers
