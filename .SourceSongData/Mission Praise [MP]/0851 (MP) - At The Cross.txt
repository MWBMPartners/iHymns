"At the cross"

ALL

I know a place,

a wonderful place,

where accused and condemned

find mercy and grace;

where the wrongs we have done

and the wrongs done to us

were nailed there with Him,

there on the cross.

Chorus:
MEN    At the cross,

WOMEN    at the cross,

ALL    He died for our sin.

MEN    At the cross,

WOMEN    at the cross,

ALL    He gave us life again.
