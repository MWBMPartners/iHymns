"Father, make us one"

1  Father, make us one,

Father, make us one,

that the world may know Thou hast sent the Son,

Father, make us one.

2  Behold how pleasant and how good it is

for brethren to dwell in unity,

for there the Lord commands the blessing,

life for evermore.
