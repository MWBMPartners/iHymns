"I’m going to raise a banner of praise"

Chorus:
I’m going to raise a banner of praise,

lifting up the name of Jesus,

songs of His grace, filling this place,

lifting up the name of Jesus.

Proclaiming in His name

good news for the poor,

proclaiming in His name

sight for the blind,

proclaiming in His name

freedom for captives.

This is the year of the Lord.

1  I want to sing, I want to proclaim,

my life is never going to be the same,

I once was lost, but now I’m found.

Jesus’ love has turned me around!

I’m going to raise a banner of praise...

2  Once my heart was stained by sin,

now by His blood I’ve been made clean,

where I was bound, I’ve been set free,

He’s placed His Holy Spirit in me.

I’m going to raise a banner of praise...

This is the year of the Lord,

this is the year of the Lord,

this is the year of the Lord.
