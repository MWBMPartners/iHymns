"Jesus, You are the radiance"

Jesus, You are the radiance

of the Father’s glory,

You are the Son,

the appointed heir,

through whom all things are made;

You are the one

who sustains all things

by Your powerful word,

You have purified us from sin,

You are exalted, O Lord,

exalted, O Lord,

to the right hand of God:

Jesus, You are...

Crowned with glory,

crowned with honour –

we worship You.
