"All the riches of His grace"

Chorus:
All the riches of His grace,

all the fulness of His blessings,

all the sweetness of His love

He gives to you, He gives to me.

1  O the blood of Jesus, (3 times)

it washes white as snow.

All the riches...

2  O the word of Jesus, (3 times)

it cleanses white as snow.

All the riches...

3  O the love of Jesus, (3 times)

it makes His body whole.

All the riches...

4  O the blood of Jesus, (3 times)

it washes white as snow.

All the riches...


Words and music by Jan Harrington
