"He is here, He is here"

1  He is here, He is here,

He is moving among us;

He is here, He is here,

as we gather in His name!

He is here, He is here,

and He wants to work a wonder;

He is here as we gather in His name.

2  He is Lord, He is Lord,

let us worship before Him;

He is Lord, He is Lord,

as we gather in His name!

He is Lord, He is Lord,

let us praise and adore Him

yesterday and today and for evermore the same.


Words and music by Jimmy Owens

Music arranged by Roland Fudge
