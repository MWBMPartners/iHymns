"You are the mighty King"

1  You are the mighty King,

the living Word;

Master of everything –

You are the Lord.

And I praise Your name,

and I praise Your name.

2  You are almighty God,

Saviour and Lord;

wonderful counsellor,

You are the Lord.

And I praise Your name,

and I praise Your name.

3  You are the Prince of peace,

Emmanuel;

everlasting Father,

You are the Lord.

And I love Your name,

and I love Your name.

4  You are the mighty King,

the living Word;

Master of everything,

You are the Lord.
