"Only You deserve the praise"

1  Hear us now, O loving God, we pray for this, our world.
O hear the words of our confession;
we have bowed to other gods and crowded out Your voice,
allowed injustice and oppression.
When will we return to You?
Father, Father, turn us back to loving You.
Only You deserve the praise, only You deserve the song,
O Lord, it all belongs to You.
Only You deserve the love, only You deserve the thanks,
O Lord, it all belongs to You.

2  For the children of this land we lift to You our hands
and ask You, Father, please have mercy.
For innocence betrayed forgive us, Lord, we pray;
and once again pour out Your Spirit.
When will we return to You?
Father, Father, turn us back to loving You.
Only You deserve the praise...

3  For the earth in deep distress, our greed, our selfishness,
O hear the words of our confession.
O teach us how to share, restore, renew, repair;
and welcome in Your new creation.
When will we return to You?
Father, Father, turn us back to loving You.
Only You deserve the praise...

Hear us now, O loving God, we pray for this our world.
O hear the words of our confession.
