"Behold the Lamb of God"

1  Behold the Lamb of God!

Behold our Saviour!

Our mighty King of kings,

Jesus.

2  Enthroned in majesty,

enthroned in splendour,

our mighty King of kings,

Jesus.

He’s the Lord of all creation,

He’s the Saviour for all nations,

He’s the Lamb enthroned in heaven,

our returning King.

Every eye at last will see him,

every knee will bow before Him,

every tongue will then confess

that Jesus Christ is Lord!

3  Reigning in victory,

reigning in glory,

our mighty King of kings,

Jesus.


Words and music by Greg Leavers
