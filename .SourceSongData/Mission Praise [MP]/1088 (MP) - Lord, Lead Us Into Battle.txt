"Lord, lead us into battle"

1  Lord, lead us into battle,

let us glorify Your name.

Holy Spirit, You have set our hearts aflame,

we will never be the same.

2  Lord, fill us with the vision

to see the Church as one.

Break our hearts, release us from our pride;

Father, glorify Your bride.

Oh, how I love You, Jesus,

what a joy to sing Your praise.

I have placed my hope in You and You alone,

I’ve found healing in Your name.

3  Lord, teach us how to worship,

may it never be in vain.

Draw us into the throne room of the King;

in Your power rule and reign.

Oh, how I love You...
