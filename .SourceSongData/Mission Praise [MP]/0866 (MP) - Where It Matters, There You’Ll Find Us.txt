"Where it matters, there you’ll find us"

1  In the streets of every city,

bringing hope and healing strife,

living out the Saviour’s pity,

caring for each precious life.

2  Through the world, in every nation,

serving Christ, with lives made whole;

in His name we speak salvation

grace for every seeking soul;

Where it matters, there you’ll find us,

where it matters, there you’ll find us

serving Jesus;

where it matters, there you’ll find us

in the service of our Lord.

3  In our daily time of praying,

thanking God for sins forgiven,

hearing what His voice is saying,

tasting now the joys of Heaven.

4  At His table, there you’ll find us

pouring wine and sharing bread;

‘Christ has died’ these gifts remind us,

‘Christ is risen from the dead’.

Where it matters...

5  God of city, and of nation,

Lord ascended, coming King;

with resounding exultation

of Your faithfulness we sing.

Where it matters...


Words by Michael Perry

Music by Chris Rolinson
