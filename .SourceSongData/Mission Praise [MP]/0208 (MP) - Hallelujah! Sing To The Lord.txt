"Hallelujah! sing to the Lord"

Hallelujah! sing to the Lord songs of praise;

we bless You, Lord,

we give to You glory due Your holy name.

We stretch out our hands,

we stretch out our hands unto You, Lord.

We lift up our voice,

we lift up our voice inviting You into this place.

Hear us, O God,

as one, we bring our praise,

a pleasing sacrifice to You,

O Ancient of Days,

Ancient of Days.
