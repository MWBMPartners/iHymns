"If My people who bear My name"

If My people who bear My name

will humble themselves and pray;

if they seek My presence

and turn their backs on their wicked ways;

then I will hear from heaven,

I’ll hear from heaven and will forgive.

I will forgive their sins

and will heal their land –

yes, I will heal their land.

Music arranged by Christopher Norton
