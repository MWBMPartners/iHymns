"Glory to You"

1  Glory to You,

glory to You;

may all that we are,

and all that we do

bring glory, Lord, to You.

2  Honour to You,

honour to You;

may all that we are,

and all that we do

bring honour, Lord, to You.

3  Blessing to You,

blessing to You;

may all that we are,

and all that we do

bring blessing, Lord, to You.
