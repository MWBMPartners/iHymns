"Everlasting"

From everlasting,

to everlasting,

You are God.

From everlasting,

to everlasting,

You are God.

1  In holiness You stand secure

through culture’s shifting sands,

unchanged by all the vanities of man.

And as the nations rise and fall,

Your sovereignty remains.

You are, You are, You are

the one true God.

From everlasting...

2  In faithfulness Your love extends

through times of turbulence,

adopting those who call upon Your name.

And every generation joins

in songs of grateful praise.

You are, You are, You are

the one true God.

From everlasting...

Eternal, immortal, invisible God.

Eternal, immortal, invisible God.
