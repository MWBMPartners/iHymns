"Would You move"

1  Could it be that God could move
into this, our neighbourhood;
O Lord, would You come?
The universe too small a place
to even give You breathing space,
O Lord, would You come?
Hear Your people cry today.
Hear from heaven, change our ways.
Lift our focus in this place:
we’re desperate for Your face,
we’re desperate for Your face.
Would You move in power,
till we cannot move?
Would You cloud our vision
to see only You?
Would You breathe through here
blowing out the dust?
Would You make Your dwelling
right here with us?

2  When the battle steals our soul,
we return to be made whole;
O Lord, would You come?
When disasters strike our faith,
we ask why, but ask for grace;
O Lord, would You come?
Hear Your people...
Would You move in power...
