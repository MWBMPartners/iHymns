"Oh yes, how I love Jesus"

1  Oh yes, how I love Jesus,

friend of the lonely, healing my pain.

Down through the ages, He never changes,

oh, I love Jesus, ever the same.

2  Oh yes, how I love Jesus,

finding the lost, He shows them the way.

He makes the blind see, He sets the bound free,

when you love Jesus, night turns to day.

3  Oh yes, how I love Jesus,

all who have wandered, lost and alone;

no condemnation, just restoration,

those who love Jesus, He welcomes home.

4  Oh yes, how I love Jesus,

hope for the hopeless, peace for the storm.

All of my mourning He turns to dancing,

oh, I love Jesus, God’s precious Son.

5  Oh yes, how I love Jesus,

He is my Saviour, He is my Lord.

Oh yes, how I love Jesus,

He is my Saviour, He is my Lord.
