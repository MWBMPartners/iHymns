"I see your face in every sunrise"

1 I see Your face in every sunrise,

the colours of the morning are inside Your eyes.

The world awakens in the light of the day,

I look up to the sky and say:

You’re beautiful.

You’re beautiful.

2 I see Your power in the moonlit night,

where planets are in motion and galaxies are bright.

We are amazed in the light of the stars;

it’s all proclaiming who You are:

You’re beautiful.

You’re beautiful.

3 I see You there, hanging on a tree;

You bled and then You died

and then You rose again for me.

Now You are sitting on Your heavenly throne,

and soon we will be coming home:

You’re beautiful.

You’re beautiful.

4 When we arrive at eternity’s shore,

where death is just a memory and tears are no more,

we’ll enter in as the wedding bells ring;

Your bride will come together and we’ll sing:

You’re beautiful.

You’re beautiful.

I see Your face, You’re beautiful,

You’re beautiful, You’re beautiful.

Chorus:
I see Your face...
I see Your face...
I see Your face...
Phil Wickham
