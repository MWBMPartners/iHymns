"Spirit of God divine"

1  Spirit of God divine,

fill this heart of mine

with holy flame,

to praise the name

of Jesus my Lord.

Fill me again,

fill me again,

fill me again,

O Spirit of the Lord.

2  Spirit of God divine,

fill this mouth of mine

with holy praise,

to set the earth ablaze

and glorify Your name.

Fill me again...

3  Spirit of God divine,

take this heart of mine

to Your throne this day;

help me, I pray,

my offering to give.

Fill me again...


Words and music by Colin Preston

Music arranged by Chris Mitchell

? 1984 Colin Preston, 81 Howth Drive, Woodley, Reading, Berkshire, UK
