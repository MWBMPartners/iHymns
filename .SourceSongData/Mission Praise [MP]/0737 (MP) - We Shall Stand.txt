"We shall stand"

Chorus:
We shall stand, with our feet on the rock;

whatever men may say, we’ll lift Your name up high –

and we shall walk through the darkest night;

setting our faces like flint,

we’ll walk into the light!

1  Lord, You have chosen me for fruitfulness

to be transformed into Your likeness:

I’m going to fight on through till I see You

face to face.

We shall stand...

2  Lord, as Your witnesses You’ve appointed us,

and with Your Holy Spirit anointed us:

and so I’ll fight on through till I see You

face to face.

We shall stand...


Words and music by Graham Kendrick
