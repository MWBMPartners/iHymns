"The Cross Still Stands"

1  For every disappointment,

for every broken heart,

for everyone in darkness, a light.

2  For every wounded person,

for every tired mind,

for hopeless situations, a hope.

The cross still stands,

the cross still towers,

His blood still cleanses,

eternally the same.

3  For everyone who’s desperate,

for everyone who’s lost,

for everyone who’s fearful, a shelter.

4  For every painful question,

for everyone’s regrets,

for every cry of ‘Why, God?’, an answer.

The cross still stands...

Chorus:
Grace and peace, mercy and joy,

all undeserved, I bow down.

Chorus:
The cross still stands...
(instrumental verse)

And I bow down,

Lord, I run to the cross again,

I bow down, I bow down.
