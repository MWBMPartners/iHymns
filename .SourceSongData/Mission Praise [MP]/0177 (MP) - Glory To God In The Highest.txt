"Glory to God in the highest"

Chorus:
Glory to God in the highest,

peace upon earth,

Jesus Christ has come to earth;

that’s why we sing,

Jesus the King,

Jesus has come for you.

1  The shepherds who were sitting there

were suddenly filled with fear;

the dark night was filled with light,

angels singing everywhere.

Glory to God...

2  The next time we hear a song

of worship from a heavenly throng,

will be when Jesus comes again,

then with triumph we’ll all sing:

Glory to God...


Words and music by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by Phil Burt

? 1986 Greg Leavers
