"Awake, awake, O Zion"

Chorus:
Awake, awake, O Zion,

come clothe yourself with strength.

Awake, awake, O Zion,

come clothe yourself with strength.

1  Put on your garments of splendour,

O Jerusalem;

come sing your songs of joy and triumph,

see that your God reigns.

Awake, awake...

2  Burst into songs of joy together,

O Jerusalem;

the Lord has comforted His people,

the redeemed Jerusalem.

Awake, awake...
