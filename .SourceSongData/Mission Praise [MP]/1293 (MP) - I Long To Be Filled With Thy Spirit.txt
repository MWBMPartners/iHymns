"I long to be filled with Thy spirit"

I long to be filled with Thy Spirit, O Lord;

completely controlled by Thee.

I desire that my life be in perfect accord

with Thine own for eternity.

My heart, life and all now to Thee I give;

O make themThine own and through me now live.

I long to be filled by Thy Spirit, O Lord,

completely controlled by Thee.

Cliff Barrows
