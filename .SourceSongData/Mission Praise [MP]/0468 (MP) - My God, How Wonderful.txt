"My God, how wonderful"

1  My God, how wonderful You are,

Your majesty how bright!

How beautiful Your mercy-seat,

in depths of burning light!

2  In awe I glimpse eternity,

O everlasting Lord;

by angels worshipped day and night,

incessantly adored!

3  O how I love You, living God,

who my heart’s longing hears,

and worship You with certain hope

and penitential tears!

4  Yes, I may love You, O my Lord,

almighty King of kings,

for You have stooped to live in me,

with joy my heart now sings:

5  How wonderful, how beautiful,

Your loving face must be,

Your endless wisdom, boundless power,

and awesome purity!


Words by Frederick William Faber

Words altered by Peter Horrobin

Words altered by Greg Leavers

Music by James Turle
