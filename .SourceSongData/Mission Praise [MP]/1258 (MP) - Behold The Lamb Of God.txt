"Behold the Lamb of God"

1 Behold the Lamb of God,

who takes away all the sin of the world.

He conquered through precious blood,

purchased us with His love;

now He has power to save.

2 Behold the champion of grace;

He bore our sin on the cross, took our place.

Our humble God gave His life,

one complete sacrifice;

now He is seated on high.

For You are worthy,

You are worthy,

Jesus, the glory of God;

for You are worthy,

You are worthy,

Jesus, the glory of God.

3 Behold our King is alive,

He’s crowned in glory and power,

clothed with light.

All heaven sings out His name

with an anthem of praise.

Jesus, the glory of God.

For you are worthy...
