"We fall down"

We fall down, we lay our crowns

at the feet of Jesus.

The greatness of mercy and love,

at the feet of Jesus.

And we cry holy, holy, holy.

And we cry holy, holy, holy.

And we cry holy, holy,

holy is the Lamb.

We fall down...

And we cry holy...
