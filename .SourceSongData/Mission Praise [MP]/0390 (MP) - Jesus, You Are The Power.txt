"Jesus, You are the power"

Jesus, You are the power,

You are the wisdom

that comes from the Lord God,

who has revealed His love.

Our faith now rests on Your power, Lord,

which Your Spirit has poured out on us.

We declare the mystery hid before the ages,

which God had planned for our glory.

For we have received a glorious inheritance,

pledged by the Spirit.

And our eyes have not seen,

and our ears have not heard,

what is in store for the hearts

of the ones who love the Lord.
