"The Spirit of the Lord"

1  The Spirit of the Lord,

the sovereign Lord, is on me,

because He has anointed me

to preach good news to the poor:

Proclaiming Jesus, only Jesus –

it is Jesus, Saviour, healer and baptizer,

and the mighty King, the victor and deliverer –

He is Lord, He is Lord, He is Lord.

2  And He has called on me

to bind up all the broken hearts,

to minister release to every

captivated soul:

Proclaiming Jesus...

3  Let righteousness arise

and blossom as a garden;

let praise begin to spring in every

tongue and nation:

Proclaiming Jesus...
