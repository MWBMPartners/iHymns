"Soon, and very soon"

1  Soon, and very soon,

we are going to see the King;

soon, and very soon,

we are going to see the King;

soon, and very soon,

we are going to see the King;

alleluia, alleluia,

we’re going to see the King!

2  No more crying there...

alleluia...

3  No more dying there...

alleluia...

Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, alleluia.

4  Soon and very soon...

alleluia...

Alleluia, alleluia, alleluia, alleluia.
