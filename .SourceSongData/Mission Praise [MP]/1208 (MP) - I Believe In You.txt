"I believe in You"

1  Most holy Father, I come to You,

knowing that You alone, You can make me whole.

My precious Jesus, I long for You

to make my heart a dwelling place for Your love.

For You alone are holy, bountiful in mercy,

forever You are worthy to receive the glory.

And I believe in You, ruler over everything.

The wonders of my God and King shine over all the earth.

And I believe in You, my Saviour, You’re amazing.

Your love is never ending, and I believe in You.

2  Thankful, so thankful, I come to You,

knowing that You alone have made a way for me.

Faithful, so faithful You are to me.

Steadfast and certain in Your love.

For You alone are holy, bountiful in mercy,

forever You are worthy to receive the glory.

And I believe in You...
