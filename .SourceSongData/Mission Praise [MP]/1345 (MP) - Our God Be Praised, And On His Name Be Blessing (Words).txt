"Our God be praised, and on His Name be blessing (words)"

1 Our God be praised,
and on His name be blessing;
how rich in mercy to redeem, how swift to save!
He heals and pardons those who come
their sin confessing;
praise be to God, who rescues from the grave!
2 High as the heavens, see His mercy towering!
He sets our sins as far as east is from the west.
Our days are brief as short-lived
summer meadows’ flowering,
yet in His love we are for ever blest.
3 May children’s children learn that love amazing,
all creatures bow before His universal throne;
His whole dominion join in everlasting praising:
glory to God, my soul, and God alone!
Timothy Dudley-Smith
