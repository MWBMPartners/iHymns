"Father, sending Your anointed Son"

1  Father, sending Your anointed

Son to save, forgive, and heal;

and, through Him, Your Holy Spirit,

to make our salvation real;

2  Look upon our ills and trouble,

and on those who suffer much.

Send Your Church the Spirit’s unction

in Christ’s name to heal and touch.

3  Grant forgiveness to the faithful;

bring to unity their prayer;

use it for Your work unhindered,

through both sacrament and care.


Words by John Richards

Music by Edward Henry Thorne
