"Open our eyes, Lord"

Open our eyes, Lord,

we want to see Jesus –

to reach out and touch Him

and say that we love Him.

Open our ears, Lord,

and help us to listen:

O open our eyes, Lord,

we want to see Jesus!


Words and music by Robert Cull

Music arranged by David Peacock
