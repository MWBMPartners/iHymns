"The Lord is King"

1  The Lord is King,

He is mighty in battle,

working wonders,

glorious in majesty.

2  The Lord is King –

so majestic in power!

His right hand

has shattered the enemy.

3  A This is my God

and I will praise Him;

B this is my God

and I will praise Him:

4  A My strength and song

and my salvation,

B my strength and song

and my salvation.

(The singers may divide at f*rAf*i and B)
