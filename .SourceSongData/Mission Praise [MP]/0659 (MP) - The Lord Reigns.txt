"The Lord reigns"

Chorus:
The Lord reigns, the Lord reigns,

He is robed in majesty,

the Lord is robed in majesty,

and He is girded with strength.

1  The Lord has established the world,

it shall never be moved;

Thy throne is established of old,

Thou art from everlasting.

The Lord reigns...

2  The floods have lifted up, O Lord,

lifted up their voice;

mightier than the thunder of the waves,

the Lord on high is mighty.

The Lord reigns...
