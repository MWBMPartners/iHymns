"Glorify Your name"

1  Father, we love You,

we worship and adore You:

glorify Your name

in all the earth.

Glorify Your name, (3 times)

in all the earth.

2  Jesus, we love You,

we worship and adore You:

glorify Your name

in all the earth.

Glorify Your name, (3 times)

in all the earth.

3  Spirit, we love You,

we worship and adore You:

glorify Your name

in all the earth.

Glorify Your name, (3 times)

in all the earth.
