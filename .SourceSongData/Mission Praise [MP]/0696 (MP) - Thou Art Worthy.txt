"Thou art worthy"

1  Thou art worthy, Thou art worthy,

Thou art worthy, O Lord.

Thou art worthy to receive glory,

glory and honour and power.

For Thou hast created, hast all things created,

for Thou hast created all things;

and for Thy pleasure they are created:

Thou art worthy, O Lord.

2  Thou art worthy, Thou art worthy,

Thou art worthy, O Lamb.

Thou art worthy to receive glory

and power at the Father’s right hand.

For Thou hast redeemed us;

hast ransomed and cleaned us,

by Thy blood setting us free.

In white robes arrayed us, kings and priests made us,

and we are reigning in Thee.


Words and music by Pauline Michael Mills
