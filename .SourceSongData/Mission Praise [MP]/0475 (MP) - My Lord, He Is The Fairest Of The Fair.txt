"My Lord, He is the fairest of the fair"

My Lord, He is the fairest of the fair,

He is the lily of the valley,

the bright and morning star;

His love is written deep within my heart,

He is the never-ending fountain

of everlasting life:

and He lives, He lives,

He lives, He lives in me.
