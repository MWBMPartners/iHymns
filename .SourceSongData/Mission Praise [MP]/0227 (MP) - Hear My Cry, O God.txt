"Hear my cry, O God"

Hear my cry, O God,

listen to my prayer;

from the ends of the earth

will I call to You.

Hear my cry, O God,

when my heart is overwhelmed;

lead me to the rock

that is higher than I.

Teach me to trust in You,

to pour out my heart to You;

You’re my help,

my refuge and my strength.

Hear my cry, O God,

listen to my prayer;

from the ends of the earth

will I call to You;

hear my cry, O God.


Words and music by Andy Silver

? 1986 Andy Silver
