"Teach me to live"

1  Teach me to live, day by day,

in Your presence, Lord;

day by day, in Your presence, Lord,

teach me to live.

2  Teach me to praise, day by day,

in Your Spirit, Lord;

day by day, in Your Spirit, Lord,

teach me to praise.

3  Teach me to love, day by day,

in Your power, Lord;

day by day, in Your power, Lord,

teach me to love.

4  Teach me to give, day by day,

from my wealth, O Lord;

day by day, from my wealth, O Lord,

teach me to give.


Words and music by Elizabeth M Dyke

? 1982 Elizabeth M Dyke, 29 Chalfont Close, Bedworth, Warks CV12 8PB
