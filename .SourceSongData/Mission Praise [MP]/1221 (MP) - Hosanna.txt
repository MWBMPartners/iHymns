"Hosanna"

1  Praise is rising,

eyes are turning to You,

we turn to You.

Hope is rising,

hearts are yearning for You,

we long for You.

’Cause when we see You,

we find strength to face the day;

in Your presence

all our fears are washed away, washed away.

Hosanna, hosanna,

You are the God who saves us,

worthy of all our praises.

Hosanna, hosanna,

come have Your way among us,

we welcome You here Lord Jesus.

2  Hear the sound of hearts

returning to You,

we turn to You.

In Your kingdom

broken lives

are made new,

You make us new.

’Cause when we see You,

we find strength to face the day;

in Your presence

all our fears are washed away, washed away.

Hosanna, hosanna...

Chorus:
Hosanna, hosanna.

Hosanna, hosanna.
