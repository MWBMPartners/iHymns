"My life is Yours"

My life is Yours, O Lord,

my life is Yours, O Lord;

so do as You will,

do what is pleasing to You,

for my life belongs to You.

Teach me the fear of the Lord,

let me see Your righteousness;

I will kneel before You

and worship Christ my King.


Words and music by Ruth Hooke

Ruth Hooke, ? 1987, 10 Bridgeway, Lostock Hall, Preston, Lancs PR5 5YJ
