"How precious, O Lord"

How precious, O Lord,

is Your unfailing love;

we find refuge

in the shadow of Your wings.

We feast, Lord Jesus,

on the abundance of Your house

and drink from Your river of delights.

With You is the fountain of life,

in Your light we see light,

with You is the fountain of life,

in Your light we see light.
