"Come to the waters"

Chorus:
Come to the waters

and I will give you rest;

come to the waters

and you will be refreshed.

1  Jesus said, ‘Come unto Me

all ye weary, heavy-laden.’

Come to the waters...

2  Jesus said, of the waters that He gave,

‘He who drinks shall never thirst again.’

Come to the waters...

3  Jesus said, ‘He who believes in Me,

out of him shall flow living waters.’

Come to the waters...

4  So with joy we shall draw water

out of wells of salvation.

Come to the waters...


Words and music by Jodi Page Clark
