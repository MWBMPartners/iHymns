"He knows my name"

I have a Maker,

He formed my heart;

before even time began

my life was in His hand.

I have a Father,

He calls me His own;

He’ll never leave me,

no matter where I go.

He knows my name,

He knows my every thought;

He sees each tear that falls

and hears me when I call.
