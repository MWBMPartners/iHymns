"I’m forgiven"

1  I get so excited, Lord,

every time I realize

I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven.

Jesus, Lord, You’ve done it all,

You’ve paid the price:

I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven.

Hallelujah, Lord,

my heart just fills with praise,

my feet start dancing, my hands rise up,

and my lips they bless Your name.

I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven.

I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven.

2  Living in Your presence, Lord,

is life itself;

I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven.

with the past behind, grace for today,

and a hope to come;

I’m forgiven, I’m forgiven.

Hallelujah...
