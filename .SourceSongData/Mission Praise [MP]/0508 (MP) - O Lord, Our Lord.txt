"O Lord, our Lord"

O Lord, our Lord,

how majestic is Your name in all the earth;

O Lord, our Lord,

how majestic is Your name in all the earth;

O Lord, we praise Your name;

O Lord, we magnify Your name.

Prince of peace, mighty God,

O Lord God almighty!
