"Light in my darkness"

1 Light in my darkness,
peace for my soul,
You are my rescue,
You never let go.
Light in my darkness...
All my hope is in You,
all my strength is in You,
with every breath
my soul will rest in You.
2 Here in my weakness,
always the same,
Your love is my shelter,
Your life is my way.
All my hope is in You...
All my hope is in You...
Constant Saviour, friend for ever,
Lord, You have my heart.
Sure foundation, never failing,
Lord, You have my heart.
Constant Saviour...
All my hope is in You...
All the earth beneath You,
all my life before You,
with every breath
my soul will rest in You,
with every breath
my soul will rest in You.

Reuben Morgan and Jason Ingram
