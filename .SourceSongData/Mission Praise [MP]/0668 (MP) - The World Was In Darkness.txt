"The world was in darkness"

1  The world was in darkness, in sin and shame;

mankind was lost, and then Jesus came.

He carried our sins to Calvary’s tree,

He hung there, and bled there, for you and me.

Thank You, Lord, for saving my soul.

Thank You, Lord, for making me whole.

Thank You, Lord, for giving to me

Thy great salvation so rich and free.

2  Lord Jesus came down from His throne on high;

ready to live and willing to die.

For all of the pain and the suffering He bore,

I’ll love Him and thank Him for evermore.

Thank You, Lord...

3  To You I surrender my all today,

the debt I owe, I ne’er could repay,

I’ll serve You with joy wherever You lead,

with this great assurance, You’ll meet my need.

Thank You, Lord...
