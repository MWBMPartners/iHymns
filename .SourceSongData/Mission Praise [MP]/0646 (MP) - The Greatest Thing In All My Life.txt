"The greatest thing in all my life"

1  The greatest thing in all my life

is knowing You;

the greatest thing in all my life

is knowing You;

I want to know You more;

I want to know You more.

The greatest thing in all my life

is knowing You.

2  The greatest thing in all my life

is loving You;

the greatest thing in all my life

is loving You;

I want to love You more;

I want to love You more.

The greatest thing in all my life

is loving You.

3  The greatest thing in all my life

is serving You;

the greatest thing in all my life

is serving You;

I want to serve You more;

I want to serve You more.

The greatest thing in all my life

is serving You.


Words and music by Mark Pendergrass
