"Jesus, how lovely You are"

Chorus:
Jesus, how lovely You are!

You are so gentle, so pure and kind,

You shine like the morning star:

Jesus, how lovely You are.

1  Alleluia, Jesus is my Lord and King;

Alleluia, Jesus forgave all my sin.

Jesus, how lovely...

3  Alleluia, Jesus is meek and lowly;

Alleluia, Jesus is pure and holy.

Jesus, how lovely...

4  Alleluia, Jesus is the bridegroom;

Alleluia, Jesus will take His bride soon.

Jesus, how lovely...
