"See His love"

Chorus:
See His love nailed to a cross,
perfect and blameless life
given as sacrifice.
See Him there all in the name of love,
broken, yet glorious,
all for the sake of us.
This is Jesus in His glory,
king of heaven, dying for me.
It is finished, He has done it.
Death is beaten, heaven beckons me.

2 Greater love no one could ever show,
mercy so undeserved,
freedom I should not know.
All my sin, all of my hidden shame,
died with Him on the cross,
eternity won for us.
This is Jesus...
Such love, such love,
such love is this for me.
(x4)
This is Jesus...

Tom Lockley
