"Hold fast, my soul"

1 Hold fast,my soul, to Him who never changes.

Eternal Love, who is the Rock of Ages.

In altering times, I need not fret or waver.

I trust the One who is the same for ever.

2 Fear not,my soul, when steered to stormy waters.

Eternal Hope, through many trials You’ve brought us.

My confidence: Your Spirit without measure.

I praise the One who is the same for ever.

3 Have faith,my soul, to speak unto the mountain.

Eternal God, deliver me from doubting.

You’re by my side in all that I endeavour,

the Faithful One who is the same for ever.

4 Awake, my soul, and give Him all the glory.

Eternal King, I worship You; You’re worthy.

I live for You and for no earthly treasure.

I serve the One who is the same for ever.

Malcokm Baxter
