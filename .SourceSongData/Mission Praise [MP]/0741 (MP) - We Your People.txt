"We Your people"

1  We Your people bow before You

broken and ashamed;

we have turned on Your creation,

crushed the life You freely gave.

2  Lord, have mercy on Your children

weeping and in fear:

for You are our God and Saviour,

Father, in Your love draw near.

3  Father, in this hour of danger

we will turn to You:

O forgive us, Lord, forgive us

and our lives and faith renew.

4  Pour Your Holy Spirit on us,

set our hearts aflame:

all shall see Your power in the nations,

may we bring glory to Your name.
