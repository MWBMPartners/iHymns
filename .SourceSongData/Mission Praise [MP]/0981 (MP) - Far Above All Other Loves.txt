"Far above all other loves"

1  Far above all other loves,

far beyond all other joys,

heaven’s blessings poured on me,

by the Holy Spirit’s power.

Love’s compelling power draws my heart into Yours;

Jesus, how I love You, You’re my friend and my Lord.

You have died and risen, so what else can I say?

How I love You, Lord, love You, Lord.

2  All ambition now has gone,

pleasing You my only goal;

motivated by Your grace,

living for eternity.

Love’s compelling power...

3  Looking with the eye of faith

for the day of Your return;

in that day I want to stand

unashamed before Your throne.

Love’s compelling power...
