"King of love and grace"

1 King of love and grace, my guardian,
all my hopes and fears are in Your hands,
I’m in Your hands.
Where You go I’ll go; show me the way.
Every step I take, be now my guide,
God on my side.
You go before me,
You’re there beside me,
and if I wander, Love will find me.
Goodness and mercy will always follow.
You go before me, my guardian.

2 When I hear You say, ‘Trust and obey’,
I will walk by faith and not by sight,
God of my life.
So let Your kingdom come, Your will be done.
All Your promises will stand for ever.
You’re my defender.
You go before me...
You’re there beside me,
and if I wander, Love will find me.
Goodness and mercy will always follow.
You go before me, my guardian.

Chorus:
You are God, our great defender,
strong in love, for ever faithful.
We are Yours and we will trust in You.
You are God...
You go before me...
…My Guardian, my guardian.

Ben Cantelon, Nick Herbert, Stu Garrad
