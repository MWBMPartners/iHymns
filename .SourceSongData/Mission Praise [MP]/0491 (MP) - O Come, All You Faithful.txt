"O come, all you faithful"

1  O come, all you faithful,

joyful and triumphant,

O come now, O come now to Bethlehem;

come and behold Him,

born the King of angels:

O come, let us adore Him,

O come, let us adore Him,

O come, let us adore Him,

Christ the Lord!

2  True God of true God,

light of light eternal,

He, who abhors not the virgin’s womb;

Son of the Father,

begotten not created:

O come, let us adore Him...

3  Sing like the angels,

sing in exultation,

sing with the citizens of heaven above,

‘Glory to God,

glory in the highest’:

O come, let us adore Him...

4  Yes, Lord, we greet You,

born that/this happy morning,

Jesus, to You be glory given!

Word of the Father,

then/now in flesh appearing:

O come, let us adore Him...


Words tr. by Frederick Oakley

Words altered by Peter Horrobin

Words altered by Greg Leavers

Music by John Francis Wade

Music arranged by William Henry Monk
