"Each perfect gift has come from You"

Chorus:
All I am I offer to You,
all I have is Yours.
My all I give away;
take this life I lay before You.

1  Each perfect gift has come from You,
yet I cling tightly.
And I pray, O Lord, release my grip,
so I give gladly.
All I am...

2  Your fingerprints reveal the plan
for this world’s story.
We have no other purpose than
to give You glory.
All I am...

Chorus:
For I am broken, but I am chosen,
for You have spoken grace to me.
Yes, I am broken, but I am chosen,
for You have spoken grace to me.
All I am...

3  Your footsteps mark the path of peace
but this road is narrow;
it leads me only to a cross,
Lord, I will follow.
