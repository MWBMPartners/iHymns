"With mercy"

1  Lord, send us to the lost, where brokenness abounds,

where we can show the Father’s heart.

Where we can feed the poor, where we can warm the cold,

and comfort those who weep and mourn.

Jesus, with Your compassion; send us to all the earth.

With mercy, with justice, Lord, send us to the lost;

with lives that resound with the message of the cross.

2  Lord, open up our eyes, to see the Father’s heart,

to know what grieves Your Spirit, Lord;

that we take up our cross, dying unto ourselves,

we do the things that please Your heart.

Jesus, with Your compassion; help us display Your love.

With mercy, with justice...

Chorus:
Whatever we do for the least, wherever we are called to go,

we will serve You faithfully.

With gifts unseen and works untold,

and blessings sown that no-one knows,

send us out to all the earth, Jesus.

With mercy, with justice...
