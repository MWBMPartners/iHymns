"I have decided to follow Jesus"

1  I have decided to follow Jesus, (3 times)

no turning back, no turning back.

2  The world behind me, the cross before me, (3 times)

no turning back, no turning back.

3  Though none go with me, I still will follow, (3 times)

no turning back, no turning back.

4  Will you decide now to follow Jesus? (3 times)

no turning back, no turning back.


Words and music by Anon

Music arranged by Cliff Barrows
