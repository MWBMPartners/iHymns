"I will rejoice, I will rejoice"

I will rejoice, I will rejoice,

I will rejoice in the Lord with my whole heart;

I will rejoice, I will rejoice,

I will rejoice in the Lord.

You anoint my head with oil,

and my cup surely overflows;

goodness and love shall follow me

all the days that I dwell in Your house.
