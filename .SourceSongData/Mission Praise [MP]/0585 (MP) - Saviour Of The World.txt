"Saviour of the world"

Chorus:
Saviour of the world,

thank You for dying on the cross.

All praise to You our risen Lord,

hallelujah! Jesus.

1  In the garden of Gethsemane Jesus knelt and prayed;

for He knew the time was near when He would be betrayed.

God gave Him the strength to cope with all that people did to hurt Him;

soldiers laughed and forced a crown of thorns upon His head.

Saviour of the world...

2  On a cross outside the city they nailed Jesus high;

innocent, but still He suffered as they watched Him die.

Nothing that the soldiers did could make Him lose control, for Jesus

knew the time to die, then, ‘It is finished!’ was His cry.

Saviour of the world...

3  Three days later by God’s power He rose up from the dead,

for the tomb could not hold Jesus: it was as He’d said;

victor over sin and death, He conquered Satan’s power; so let us

celebrate that Jesus is alive for evermore.

Saviour of the world...


Words and music by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by Phil Burt

? 1986 Greg Leavers
