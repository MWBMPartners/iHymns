"Jesus, closer than a friend"

1  Jesus, closer than a friend,

Jesus, with me to the end,

Jesus, taking all my pain,

wiping all my tears away,

turning them to healing rain.

For nothing can separate us

from the love of God,

not life or death, angelic powers,

not anything in all creation.

Nothing can separate us

from the love of God,

the love we have in Jesus, our Lord.

2  Jesus, dearer than all life,

Jesus, lamb of sacrifice,

Jesus, watching over me,

every day, in every way

You make us more than conquerors.

For nothing can separate us...

The love we have in Jesus, our Lord,

the love we have in Jesus, our Lord.
