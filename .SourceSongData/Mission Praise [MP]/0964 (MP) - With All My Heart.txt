"With all my heart"

With all my heart

I’ll sing praises to You my King,

with all my being,

exalt Your name!

Words never can express

my heartfelt thankfulness,

but this I now confess,

‘I love You, Lord.’

With all my heart...

‘I’ll serve You, Lord.’
