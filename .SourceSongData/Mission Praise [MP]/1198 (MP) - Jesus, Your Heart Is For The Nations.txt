"Jesus, Your heart is for the nations"

1  Jesus, Your heart is for the nations,
Your grace extends to every land,
Your love speaks in any language,
in mercy You still stretch out Your hand.

2  When I spend time in Your presence,
I catch Your passion for the lost,
Your Spirit lets me hear Your heart-cry,
‘Who will go, whatever the cost?’
Whenever, however, wherever You send me,
Jesus, I know that forever You’re with me,
Whenever, however, wherever, Lord, use me,
I lay down my life for Your glory;
Jesus, for one more soul.

3  Being a friend to the one who’s lonely,
a comforter to the one who grieves,
showing Your love to the outcast
reaching the one in their need.

4  Now Your Spirit is upon me,
I’ll preach Your good news to the poor,
proclaiming freedom to the captives,
the deaf will hear, the blind will see once more.
