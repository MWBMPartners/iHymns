"Fill the place, Lord, with Your glory"

Fill the place, Lord, with Your glory,

at this gathering of Your own;

reign in sovereign grace and power

from Your praise-surrounded throne.

Fill the place, Lord, with Your glory,

at this gathering of Your own;

we exalt You, we adore You,

thankful hearts now join as one.

You’re the Christ, the King of glory,

Father’s well-belovèd Son.

Fill the place, Lord, with Your glory,

at this gathering of Your own.
