"I sing a new song"

1  I sing a new song to the Lord, my God,

I lift my voice to Jesus, the King;

and I worship You, I worship You,

I worship You, I worship You.

2  I bow down my face at the footstool of the Lamb,

I lay down my life at the altar of God;

and I worship You, I worship You,

I worship You, I worship You.
