"Jesus, take Your rightful place"

1  Jesus, take Your rightful place in my life.

I give You complete control.

As I lay my life afresh before You,

cleanse me and make me whole.

2  Jesus, take Your rightful place in my heart.

Be seated on the throne.

As I lift my voice in adoration,

I worship You alone.
