"Because Your love is better than life"

1  Because Your love is better than life,

with my lips I will glorify You;

I will praise You as long as I live,

in Your name I lift my hands.

2  Because Your Son has given me life,

with my lips I will glorify You,

I will praise You as long as I live,

in Your name I lift my hands.

3  Because Your Spirit is filling my life,

with my lips I will glorify You,

I will praise You as long as I live,

in Your name I lift my hands.

4  Because Your love is better than life,

with my lips I will glorify You,

I will praise You as long as I live,

in Your name I lift my hands.
