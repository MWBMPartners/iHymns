"To Him who is able to keep us"

To Him who is able to keep us,

to keep us from falling away,

who’ll bring us, spotless and joyful,

into God’s presence one day.

To the only God our Saviour,

through Jesus Christ our Lord

be glory, majesty, might and power,

now, always – amen.


Words and music by Andy Silver

? 1985 Andy Silver
