"Lord, have mercy on us"

Lord, have mercy on us,

come and heal our land;

cleanse with Your fire,

heal with Your touch:

humbly we bow

and call upon You now,

O Lord, have mercy on us,

O Lord, have mercy on us.

Lord, have mercy on us...

O Lord, have mercy on us.
