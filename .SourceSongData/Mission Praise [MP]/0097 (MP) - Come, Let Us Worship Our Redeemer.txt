"Come, let us worship our Redeemer"

Chorus:
Come, let us worship our Redeemer,

let us bow down before His throne;

come, let us kneel before our maker:

holy is His name.

1  Come into His presence with thanksgiving,

make a joyful noise;

for the Lord is a great God:

King above all gods.

Come, let us worship...

2  We are the people of His pasture,

the sheep of His hand:

for Christ the Lord is our shepherd,

He will lead us home.

Come, let us worship...

3  All praises be to God the Father,

praise to Christ His Son;

praise be to God the Holy Spirit:

bless the Three-in-One!

Come, let us worship...


Words by Sarah Turner-Smith

Music by Paul Herrington

Music arranged by Phil Burt
