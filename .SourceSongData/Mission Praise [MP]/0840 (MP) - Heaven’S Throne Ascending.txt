"Heaven’s throne ascending"

1  Heaven’s throne ascending,

death’s dominion ending,

Christ the strong to save!

Now in glory seated,

work on earth completed,

risen from the grave!

Join to praise through all our days

Christ the Lord of love who sought us,

and in dying bought us.

2  Powers of darkness broken,

earth from sleep awoken,

and to life reborn!

From our nature’s prison

we with Christ are risen

by that Easter morn.

Join to sing our glorious King,

risen, reigning, high ascending,

Lord of life unending!


Words by Timothy Dudley-Smith

Music adapted by Johann Crüger

Music arranged by Johann Sebastian Bach
