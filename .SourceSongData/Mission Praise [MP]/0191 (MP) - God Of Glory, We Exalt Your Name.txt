"God of glory, we exalt Your name"

God of glory, we exalt Your name,

You who reign in majesty;

we lift our hearts to You

and we will worship,

praise, and magnify

Your holy name.

In power resplendent

You reign in glory;

eternal King,

You reign for ever:

Your word is mighty,

releasing captives,

Your love is gracious –

You are my God.


Words and music by David Fellingham
