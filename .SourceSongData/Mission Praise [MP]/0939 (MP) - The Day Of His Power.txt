"The day of His power"

1  The Lord is exalted at God’s right hand,

His banner unfurling across the land.

All power and dominion shall kneel at His throne,

for high in the heavens stands Jesus alone.

Let us rejoice at the coming of the King,

let us rejoice for the victory He brings,

lift up our heads, and let us be willing

in the day of His power.

2  He’s raised up an army, the weak and the lost.

He’s called us to follow, accepting the cost,

and if we are faithful and trust His commands,

He’ll pour out His Spirit, together we’ll stand.

Let us rejoice...
