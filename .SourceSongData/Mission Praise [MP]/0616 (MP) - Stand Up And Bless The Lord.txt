"Stand up and bless the Lord"

Stand up and bless the Lord your God,

stand up and bless the Lord;

His name is exalted above all names;

stand up and bless the Lord.

For our God is good to us,

always ready to forgive;

He is gracious and merciful,

slow to anger and very kind.

So, stand up and bless the Lord your God,

stand up and bless the Lord;

stand up and bless the Lord your God,

stand up.


Words and music by Andy Silver

? 1986 Andy Silver
