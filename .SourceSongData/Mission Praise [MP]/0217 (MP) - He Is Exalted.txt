"He is exalted"

He is exalted,

the King is exalted on high;

I will praise Him.

He is exalted,

for ever exalted

and I will praise His name!

He is the Lord;

for ever His truth shall reign.

Heaven and earth

rejoice in His holy name.

He is exalted,

the King is exalted on high.


Words and music by Twila Paris
