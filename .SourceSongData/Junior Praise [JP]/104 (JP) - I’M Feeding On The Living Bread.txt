"I’m feeding on the living bread"

I’m feeding on the living bread,

I’m drinking at the fountain head;

for all who drink, so Jesus said,

will never, never thirst again.

What, never thirst again?

No! never thirst again.

What, never thirst again?

No! never thirst again.

For all who drink, so Jesus said,

will never, never thirst again.
