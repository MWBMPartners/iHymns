"In spirit and in truth"

even if I’m feeling down, I’ll praise You anyway.

If I’m feeling lonely, if I’m feeling bad,

I’ll think about the things You’ve done, and even when I’m sad:

I will worship You in spirit and in truth,

I will worship You in spirit and in truth,

I will worship You in spirit and in truth,

I will worship You in spirit and in truth.

help me not to forget You, Lord, or keep You out of sight.

I don’t want to leave You out, not even for a day,

I want to see Your hand at work and hear the words You say.
