"Children of God’s family"

Chorus:
Heavenly Father, we would sing out Your praise,

You’re everything a Father should be.

Made us Your sons and daughters,

made us Your own, children of God’s family.

1  We older ones say, ‘Younger ones,

we’ll love and care for you.’

We young ones say to older ones,

‘Our love respects you too.’

We love you.

Heavenly Father...

2  Father, as a family

we will live forever.

Your Church is made of all ages

who love to be together.

We love you.

Heavenly Father...


Words and music by Ian Smale
