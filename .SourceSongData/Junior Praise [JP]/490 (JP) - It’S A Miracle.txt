"It’s a miracle"

1  What drives the stars without making a sound?

Why don’t they crash when they’re spinning around?

What holds me up when the world’s upside down?

I know it’s a miracle!

Who tells the ocean where to stop on the sand?

What keeps the water back from drowning the land?

Who makes the rules I don’t understand?

I know it’s a miracle!

It’s a miracle just to know

God is with me wherever I go.

It’s a miracle as big as can be,

that He can make a miracle of me!

2  Who shows the birds how to make a good nest?

How can the geese fly so far without rest?

Why do the ducks go south and not west?

I know it’s a miracle!

What makes a brown seed so tiny and dry

burst into green and grow up so high?

And shoot out blossoms of red by and by?

I know it’s a miracle!

It’s a miracle...

3  When a spring makes a brook and a brook makes a stream,

the stream makes the river water fresh as can be.

Who puts the salt in when it gets to the sea?

I know it’s a miracle!

There are thousands of people in cities I see,

the world must be crowded as crowded can be;

but God knows my name and He cares about me.

I know it’s a miracle!


Words by William J Gaither
