"From my knees to my nose"

From my knees to my nose,


from my head to my toes,


does God know all about me?


If I’m happy or sad,


if I’m good or I’m bad,


does God know all about me?


The answer is yes


and He loves me the best,


though He knows everything about me.


The answer is yes


and He loves me the best,


and He knows that my name is... (shout name)
