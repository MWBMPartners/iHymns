3  All of us have different gifts and abilities,


which we need to learn how to share.


We can all take part in building the fellowship,


showing the world we care.


Always speaking truthfully, harbouring no enmity,


demonstrating brotherly love;


working for Him faithfully, joined in perfect unity,


demonstrating brotherly love.
