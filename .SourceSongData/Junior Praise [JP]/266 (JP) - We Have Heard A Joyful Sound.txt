"We have heard a joyful sound"

3  Let the nations hear God’s voice:


Jesus saves!


So that they can then rejoice:


Jesus saves!


Shout salvation full and free


that every land may hear God’s praise –


this our song of victory:


Jesus saves!


Words by Priscilla Jane Owens
