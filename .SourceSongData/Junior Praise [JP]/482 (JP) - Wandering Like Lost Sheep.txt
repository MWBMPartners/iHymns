"Wandering like lost sheep"

Wandering like lost sheep, we were going our own way,


when Jesus the good shepherd found us, led us home,


laid down His life before us that we might all be saved.


We now belong to Jesus, we now belong to Him.


We are His sheep, we are His sheep,


we hear His voice, and follow Him.


We are His sheep, we are His sheep,


we hear His voice, and follow Him.
