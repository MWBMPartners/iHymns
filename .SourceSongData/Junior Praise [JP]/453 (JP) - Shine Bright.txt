"Shine bright"

Shine bright, dazzle, dazzle, shine bright, dazzle, dazzle.


The light of Jesus is shining bright;


shine bright, dazzle, dazzle, shine bright, dazzle, dazzle.

His life of love;


His life of light;


shines clear and bright in the darkest night.
