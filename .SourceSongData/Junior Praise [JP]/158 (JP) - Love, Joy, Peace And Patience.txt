"Love, joy, peace and patience"

Love, joy, peace and patience, kindness,


goodness, meekness, faith, self-control.


These are the fruit of God’s Holy Spirit


and against such there is no law.


Those who belong to Christ should now live this way,


walking in the Spirit each day,


so praise Him, praise Him, give Him all the glory,


walking in the Spirit each day.


Words and music by Andy Silver
