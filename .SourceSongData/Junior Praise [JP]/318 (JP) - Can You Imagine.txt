"Can you imagine"

2  Can you imagine how it feels to have


a Friend who never slumbers, never sleeps?


Can you believe that when He comes into


your heart and says He’ll live there, it’s for keeps?




Well, this experience is not imagination;


it’s a fact, oh yes, it’s true.


And I just can’t keep it to myself;


I’ll pass it on to you,


and you, and you, and you.


Words and music by Jeanette Smart
