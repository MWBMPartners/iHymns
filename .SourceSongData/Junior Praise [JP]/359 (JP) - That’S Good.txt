"That’s good"

1  He made the water wet,

He made the land stay dry.

He put twinkle in the stars

and blue in the sky.

And when He was sure

it worked as it should,

God looked at His world and said,

‘That’s good!’

Good, good, good! He said, ‘That’s good!’

Good, good, good! God looked at His world and said,

‘That’s good!’

2  He put a touch of wag

in a puppy dog’s tail,

then put a little slow

in a silly old snail.

And when He was sure

it worked as it should,

God looked at His world and said,

‘That’s good!’

Good, good, good...

Chorus:
That’s good, that’s good,

(shouted) that’s good!
