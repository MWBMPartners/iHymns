"Let’s go and tell our friends"

Chorus:
Let’s go and tell our friends that Jesus cares;

let’s go and tell our friends that Jesus cares.

We’ve got to go out and tell how Jesus died for them;

to tell them how He loves them; (that’s right!)

to tell them He’ll forgive them; (that’s true!)

to tell them He’ll be with them; (Amen!)

to tell them that He wants to be their friend.
