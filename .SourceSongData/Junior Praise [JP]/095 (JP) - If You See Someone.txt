"If you see someone"

2  If Jesus sees you lying in the road,


He won’t leave you there, he’ll give you a hand.


If Jesus sees you crying in the road,


He won’t leave you there, he’ll give you a hand.


Doesn’t matter who you are;


you might be a tramp or a movie star.


Just remember, whoever you are,


that He sees you lying there,


and He sees you crying there.
