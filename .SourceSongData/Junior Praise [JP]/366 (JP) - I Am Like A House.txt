"I am like a house"

I am like a house with two windows and a door,


but underneath I have two legs that reach down to the floor;


two ears to hear what’s happening and a thatched roof up above,


but right inside God’s given me a heart that’s full of love.


So jump up and turn around


and thank the Lord with joyful sound.


So jump up and turn around


and thank the Lord, and now sit down!
