"Sometimes I’m naughty"

1  Sometimes I’m naughty,

I know I’ve been bad;

I say such unkind things

and make people sad.

Father, I know I’ve done wrong;

Lord, please forgive me I pray.

I want to say,

I want to say

that I’m so sorry, Lord.

2  I’m rude to my family,

I want my own way.

I don’t show them kindness

or do what they say.

Father, I know...

3  In love You forgive me,

I’m glad I’m Your child.

Your Spirit lives in me

to change me inside.

Father, help me today;

help me to please You, I pray.

I want to say,

I want to say

how much I love You, Lord.


Words and music by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by Phil Burt

? 1990 Greg Leavers
