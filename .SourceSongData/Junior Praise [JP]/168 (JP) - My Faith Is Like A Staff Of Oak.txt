"My faith is like a staff of oak"

2  My faith is like a staff of oak,


O let me on it lean!


My faith, it is a sharpened sword,


may falsehood find it keen!


Now fill me with Your Spirit, Lord,


teach and change me through Your word,


and by Your love may I be stirred,


as all true saints have been.


Words by Thomas Toke Lynch
