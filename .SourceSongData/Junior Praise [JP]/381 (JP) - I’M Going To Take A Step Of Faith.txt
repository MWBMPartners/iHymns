"I’m going to take a step of faith"

1  I’m going to take (clap clap) a step of faith,

I’m going to put my trust in the Lord.

He has the power to carry me through.

If I listen carefully, He’ll tell me what to do.

I’m going to take (clap clap) a step of faith,

and put my trust in the Lord.

2  I’m going to take (clap clap) a step of faith,

I’m going to put my trust in the Lord.

He made a promise He loves me so,

wherever He might lead me, He’ll never let me go.

I’m going to take (clap clap) a step of faith,

and put my trust in the Lord.
