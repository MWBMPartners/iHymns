"It’s a song of praise"

Chorus:
It’s a song of praise,

it’s a song of thankfulness;

it’s a song of joy,

for every girl and boy.

1  God gave me my hands,

God gave me my voice;

God gave me a thankful heart,

so now I can rejoice.

It’s a song of praise...

2  God gave me my feet,

God gave me my arms;

the life and love He gives to me

makes me want to dance.

It’s a song of praise...
