"Old man Noah"

4  For forty days they were afloat –


pitter patter, pitter patter, splosh!


But they were safe inside God’s boat –


pitter patter, pitter patter, splosh!


So trust God here, believe God there,


here believe, there trust,


everywhere believe God;


wail, wail here...


flush, flush here...


‘ha ha’ here...


saw, saw here...
