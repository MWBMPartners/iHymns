"When we look up"

When we look up to the sky


and we see the sparrows fly,


let’s remember that Jesus knows them all.


When we see the lovely trees


and the flowers and the leaves,


let’s remember that Jesus made them all.


J.E.S.U.S. C.A.R.E.S.


Jesus cares for all the things He made:


that means you and me,


our friends and family.


Thank You, Jesus, for caring for me.
