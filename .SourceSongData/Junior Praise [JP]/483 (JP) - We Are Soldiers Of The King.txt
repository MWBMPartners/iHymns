"We are soldiers of the King"

We are soldiers of the King,


of His victory we will sing;


living every hour by the Spirit’s power,


marching in the name of Jesus.


Enemies are all around,


as we praise they’re losing ground;


trusting in God’s word – it’s a mighty sword –


forever friendly, faithful followers fighting for the King.


Words and music by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by Phil Burt
