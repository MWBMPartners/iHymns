"All you have to do"

All you have to do is to ask the Lord


to forgive the wrong things you have done.


Tell Him that you’re sorry you have hurt Him,


and then believe that Jesus is God’s Son.


He died on the cross to be your Saviour,


rose from the dead to be your special friend.


Ask Him in your heart, make a brand new start.


Love and serve Him till your life shall end.
