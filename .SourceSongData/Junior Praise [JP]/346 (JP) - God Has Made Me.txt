"God has made me"

2  Help me, Lord, to understand


that I’m a child who’s loved by You.


You’ll protect me, and be with me


in the things I have to do.


Thank You, Lord, that I can trust You,


thank You for the love You bring.


Thank You that You’ll never leave me;


Father God, Your praise I sing.
