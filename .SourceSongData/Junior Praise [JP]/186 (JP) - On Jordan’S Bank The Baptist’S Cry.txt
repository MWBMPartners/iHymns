"On Jordan’s bank the Baptist’s cry"

1  On Jordan’s bank the Baptist’s cry

announces that the Lord is nigh;

come then and listen for he brings

glad tidings from the King of kings.

2  Then cleansed be every heart from sin;

make straight the way for God within;

prepare we in our hearts a home,

where such a mighty guest may come.

3  For You are our salvation, Lord,

our refuge and our great reward;

without Your grace we waste away,

like flowers that wither and decay.

4  To Him who left the throne of heaven

to save mankind, all praise be given;

to God the Father, voices raise,

and Holy Spirit, let us praise.


Words by Charles Coffin

Words tr. by John Chandler

Words altered by Peter Horrobin

Words altered by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by William Henry Havergal
