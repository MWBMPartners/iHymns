"Good old Daniel"

Chorus:
There once was a man called Daniel


(good old Daniel).


And Daniel prayed three times a day


(good old Daniel).


But the King’s decree said, ‘Worship me!’


(poor old Daniel).


But Daniel would not bend the knee!


(good old Daniel).


So the gates went ‘crash’ (crash),


and the locks went ‘click’ (click),


and the lions began to roar,


and the lions began to roar.


But they couldn’t eat Daniel if they tried


(good old Daniel),


because the Lord was on his side


(good old Daniel).
