"I’ve come to a time"

1  I’ve come to a time when I must change,

a time when I have to choose,

to order my own life, or follow Christ,

and gain what I cannot lose.

My Lord, I come,

I’m giving my life to You.

Fill me with Your Spirit,

and make over my heart anew.

2  The ways of the past, I leave, I leave,

my life in His hands I place.

His strength will uphold me through anything,

sufficient for me His grace.

My Lord...

3  The wrong that I’ve done, I now confess,

I know that He will forgive.

His love is far greater than all my sin,

He’ll teach me the way to live.

My Lord...

4  From love of myself, I turn, I turn,

to serving the King of kings.

My life will be filled with the love of Christ,

whatever the future brings.

My Lord...

5  I’ve come to a time when I must change,

a time when I have to choose,

to order my own life, or follow Christ,

and gain what I cannot lose.


Words and music by Gillian E Hutchinson

Music arranged by Phil Burt
