"I’m going to set my heart"

I’m going to set my heart

on the precious word of God;

I’m going to feed my heart

on His many promises.

His word helps me in trouble,

it teaches me to praise.

I don’t want to live with a hungry heart;

I’m going to feed on the word of God.


Words and music by Greg Leavers

Music arranged by Christopher Hayward
