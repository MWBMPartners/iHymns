"Thank You for the love"

Thank You for the love that our mums give to us each day;


thank You for the help and the care that they bring our way.


Lord, we thank You for everything they do,


show us how to help them too.


Show us how to live, teaching us to appreciate;


show us how to live so that we don’t infuriate.


Lord, we ask that in everything they do,


may our mums be blessed by You.


Words and music by Andy Silver
