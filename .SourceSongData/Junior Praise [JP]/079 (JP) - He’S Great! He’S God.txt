"He’s great! He’s God"

1  He’s great! He’s God!

Jesus Christ is Lord.

He’s great! He’s God!

Trust His word.

2  His word is truth,

for He cannot lie.

His word is truth,

to live by.

3  His love is strong,

and will never end.

His love is strong,

praise His name.

4  He lives evermore

as the King of kings.

He lives evermore!

Worship Him.


Words by Peter Horrobin

Words by Greg Leavers

Music by Phil Burt
