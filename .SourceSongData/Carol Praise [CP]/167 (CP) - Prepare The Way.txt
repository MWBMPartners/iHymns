"Prepare the way"

1  Prepare the way

for the Lord;

make straight his path

in the wilderness –

a highway for our God:

every valley

shall be raised up,

and every mountain

be made low;

and the glory of the Lord will be revealed,

and the glory of the Lord will be revealed.

2  Do you not know?

Have you not heard?

He sits enthroned

above the earth

and stretches out the heavens:

lift up your voice

and with a shout,

come, lift it up:

don’t be afraid –

and the glory of the Lord will be revealed,

and the glory of the Lord will be revealed,

and the glory of the Lord will be revealed.
