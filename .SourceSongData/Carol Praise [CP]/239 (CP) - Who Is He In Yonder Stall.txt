"Who is he in yonder stall"

1  Who is he in yonder stall

at whose feet the shepherds fall?

It’s the Lord – O, wondrous story!

It’s the Lord, the king of glory!

At his feet we humbly fall –

crown him, crown him Lord of all!

2  Who is he in deep distress

fasting in the wilderness?

It’s the Lord...

3  Who is he to whom they bring

all the sick and sorrowing?

It’s the Lord...

4  Who is he the gathering throng

greet with loud triumphant song?

It’s the Lord...

5  Who is he that on the tree

dies in shame and agony?

It’s the Lord...

6  Who is he that from the grave

comes to heal and help and save?

It’s the Lord...

7  Who is he that from his throne

rules through all the worlds alone?

It’s the Lord...


Words and music by Benjamin Russell Hanby
