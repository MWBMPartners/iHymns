"All for a baby"

1  Shepherds heard the angels sing,

saw their brightness in the sky,

went to find a new-born child

in Bethlehem, a town nearby.

All for a baby, born in a barn

where animals lay,

sleeping on the hay.

All for a baby, born to be

the Lord of all we see,

of you and me.

2  Wise men came with precious gifts,

myrrh and frankincense and gold,

offered them to a new-born child

of whom the prophets had foretold.

All for a baby...

3  This all happened long ago,

yet we celebrate his birth:

God appeared as a new-born child

and lived in human form on earth.

All for a baby...
