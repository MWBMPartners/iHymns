"Angel came to Mary"

1  Angel came to Mary and said,

‘Do not be afraid,

God’s gonna give you a son,

and his kingdom will never fade;

he will be the Saviour.’

2  God he spoke to Joseph and said,

‘Do not be afraid.

This is the work of the Spirit,

and Jesus will be his name.’

We sing with the angels:

Glory to God, glory in the highest

and peace on earth to those he loves.

Glory to God, glory in the highest

and peace on earth to those he loves.

3  Shepherds saw the angels who said,

‘Do not be afraid.

In Bethlehem there’s a baby,

asleep in a bed of hay.

He will be your saviour.’

4  Jesus came from heaven

and he’s still with us today.

He says ‘I’ll always be with you’,

so do not be afraid.

We sing with the angels:

Glory to God, glory in the highest

and peace on earth to those he loves.

Glory to God, glory in the highest

and peace on earth to those he loves.
