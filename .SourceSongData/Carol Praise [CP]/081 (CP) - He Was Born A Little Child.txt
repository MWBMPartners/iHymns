"He was born a little child"

1  He was born a little child when he came to earth;

angels in the heavens above told us of his birth.

Mother Mary laid him in a cattle stall –

little baby Jesus who was Lord of all.

2  Shepherds and their quiet sheep saw the angel bright;

shepherds heard the angels singing in the night.

Mother Mary...

3  In the hills they left the lambs and the sleeping sheep;

down to Bethlehem they came, Jesus Christ to seek.

Mother Mary...

4  ‘Shepherds, you have run from afar, breathless, breathless still:

who is caring for your sheep on the starlit hill?’

Mother Mary...

5  ‘Shall we not adore him, lying in the hay?

Look, our saviour Jesus, born for us today!’

Mother Mary...
