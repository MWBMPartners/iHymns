"Jesus was born in a stable"

1  Jesus was born in a stable,

there was no room in the inn;

he had a stall for a cradle,

that was good enough for him.

2  Cattle asleep in the corner,

Joseph kept watch from within.

Can you imagine the sorrow?

That was good enough for him.

3  No kingly robes for his vesture,

no royal hall for this one;

but Mary cradled her treasure,

for he was God’s dear Son.

4  Jesus was born in a stable,

there was no room in the inn;

he has a stall for a cradle,

that was good enough for him.

And that was good enough for him.


Words and music by Peter Chesters
