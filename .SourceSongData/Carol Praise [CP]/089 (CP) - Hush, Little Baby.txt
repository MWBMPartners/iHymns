"Hush, little baby"

Chorus:
Hush, little baby; peace, little boy

slumbering in the hay;

dream while we carol tidings of joy,

Jesus of Christmas Day.

1  Angels will tell news of good cheer,

glory will light the sky;

shepherds will kneel wondering here,

worshipping God most high.

Hush, little baby...

2  Joseph will guard, Mary will smile,

holding in sweet embrace

heaven’s true Lord, here for a while,

Jesus, God’s gift of grace.

Hush, little baby...

3  Wise men from far, soon they will come,

worship and gifts they bring:

incense for prayer, myrrh for a tomb,

gold to reveal a king.

Hush, little baby...


Words by Michael Perry

Music arranged by David Peacock
