"Go, tell it on the mountain"

Chorus:
Go, tell it on the mountain,

over the hills and everywhere;

go tell it on the mountain,

that Jesus Christ is born!

1  The shepherds from the hillside

gave worship and adored;

they saw the Babe of Bethlehem

was Jesus Christ the Lord:

Go, tell...

2  The men from eastern countries

came later there to bring

their gifts of loving homage

to Jesus Christ the King:

Go, tell...

3  A road that Jews and Gentiles,

both rich and poor have trod –

it led them all to Bethl’em,

to Christ, the Son of God:

Go, tell...

4  The learnèd and the simple

come to the manger-stall;

he joins us in his family,

the saviour of us all!

Go, tell...
