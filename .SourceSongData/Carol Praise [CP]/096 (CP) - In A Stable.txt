"In a stable"

1  In a stable, in a manger,

lies a baby – our true saviour:

hear the carol that we sing you,

and the tidings that we bring you.

There’s a light in the heavens this morning

and a song for the angels to sing;

in a stable, in a manger,

lies a baby – our true saviour.

2  There the virgin mother Mary

tends her infant – oh so gently;

and the beauty of the Godhead

shines around him – her belovèd.

There’s a light...

3  Let the mighty faint and tremble

at the triumph of the humble;

and the guilty leave their sighing

where the sinner’s hope is lying.

There’s a light...


Words by Michael Perry
