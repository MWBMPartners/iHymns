"Eternal Word"

1  Eternal Word, beyond creation’s story,

the central focus of the Father’s plan,

you were the One with God in the beginning

before the worlds of time and space began.

2  Eternal Light, the gift of life bestowing,

in whom the cosmos meets both source and end,

you are the light no darkness can extinguish,

the light which darkness cannot comprehend.

3  Though others came to herald your arrival,

you are the Word their words could scarcely tell:

eternal Light, your splendour shone among us;

eternal Life, you came with us to dwell.

4  Unrecognised, you came among your people;

so many proved reluctant to believe;

yet still you call us to become God’s children –

what finer gift could human hearts receive?

5  Eternal Life who shared our mortal journey,

eternal Light whose glory floods our gaze,

eternal Word whose grace and truth astound us,

eternal God, receive our heartfelt praise!


Words by Martin E Leckebusch
