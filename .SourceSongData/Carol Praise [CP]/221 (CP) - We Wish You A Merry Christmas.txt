"We wish you a merry Christmas"

1  We wish you a merry Christmas,

we wish you a merry Christmas,

we wish you a merry Christmas,

and a happy new year!

Good tidings we bring

of Jesus your king;

we wish you a merry Christmas

and a happy new year!

2  We’ll sing you a Christmas carol,

we’ll sing you a Christmas carol,

we’ll sing you a Christmas carol,

so quickly draw near!

Good tidings...

3  We’ll tell you the Christmas story,

we’ll tell you the Christmas story,

we’ll tell you the Christmas story,

so make sure you hear!

Good tidings...

4  We wish you a merry Christmas,

we wish you a merry Christmas,

we wish you a merry Christmas,

and a happy new year!

Good tidings...
