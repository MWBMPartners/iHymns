"I’ve waited long"

1  I’ve waited long, said Simeon;

today it has come true –

the promise made so long ago

is now fulfilled in you.

The old has gone,

the new has come.

2  The future is upon us now;

the Law which kept us right

is ancient history now as we

emerge into the light.

The old has gone,

the new has come.

3  The People of the Covenant

in whom the promise grew

will share their secret – now the hope

of Gentile as of Jew.

The old has gone,

the new has come.

4  Now custom and convention’s hold

will separate no more,

nor culture keep the nations in

perpetual state of war.

The old has gone,

the new has come.

5  It’s time to go, the old man said;

a sign is given today,

a light to comfort and reveal,

to challenge and to free.

The old has gone,

the new has come.


Words by Douglas Galbraith

Music by Tam Lane

Music Optional accompaniment by Noël Tredinnick
