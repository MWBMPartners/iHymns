"This night a miracle happened"

Chorus:
This night a miracle happened –

a boy child was born.

Go spread the news: from today

a new era will dawn.

1  Glorious things tonight are happening –

who can disbelieve their sight?

royal kings will pay him homage,

yes, coming to find this town tonight!

For, this night...

2  Angels sing their heavenly choruses

to the babe, the King of kings –

he who is the truth and glory,

a light to the darkness he will bring.

For, this night...
