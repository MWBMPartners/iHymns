"Down from the height"

1  Down from the height of his glory he came,

willingly leaving his rightful domain:

Jesus was born in the image of man;

love was his motive and mercy his aim.

2  All through those days his resolve was the same

Jesus the servant, the sharer of pain:

perfect obedience, the path of disdain,

down to a death of derision and shame.

3  Now God has granted him honour and fame,

taken him up to the highest to reign:

‘Jesus is Lord!’ every voice shall maintain,

all of creation shall bow to his name.


Words by Michael Perry
