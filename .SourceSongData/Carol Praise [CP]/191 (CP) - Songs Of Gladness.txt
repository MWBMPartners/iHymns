"Songs of gladness"

1  Songs of gladness,

songs of gladness

let us sing,

let us sing!

Glory to our Saviour,

glory to our Saviour

and our king,

and our king!

2  Joyful tidings,

joyful tidings

ring, bells, ring;

ring, bells, ring!

Sound aloud his praises,

sound aloud his praises:

Ding-dong, ding,

ding-dong, ding!


Words by Michael Perry

Music arranged by David Peacock
