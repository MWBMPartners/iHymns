"Empty he came"

1  Empty he came

as a man to our race,

equal with God

yet forsaking his place –

humbly he served in our world,

humbly he served in our world.

2  Lowlier still,

he was willing to die

nailed to a cross

as the people passed by –

bravely he died in our world,

bravely he died in our world.

3  Raised by our God

for us all to revere,

given a name

that shall stand without peer –

honoured as Lord in our world,

honoured as Lord in our world.

4  Give us that mind

that refuses to claim

even our rights,

make our outlook the same –

humbly to serve in our world,

humbly to serve in our world.


Words by Gavin Reid
