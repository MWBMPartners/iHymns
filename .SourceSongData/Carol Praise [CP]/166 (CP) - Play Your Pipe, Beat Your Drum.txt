"Play your pipe, beat your drum"

Chorus:
Play your pipe, beat your drum,

sing to Christ the Saviour;

come and worship, come and worship,

worship Christ our king!

1  Shepherds come a-dancing,

bring your pipe and drum;

come and see the baby –

run, run, run!

Play your pipe...

2  Peaceful in the manger

see him lying there,

gently gather round him –

Oh take care!

Play your pipe...

3  Listen to the angels,

can you hear them sing –

welcoming the saviour?

He’s our king!

Play your pipe...

4  Come to us, Lord Jesus,

come to us again,

in our hearts we’ll keep you,

there to reign!

Play your pipe...


Words and music by Judy Davies

Music arranged by David Peacock
