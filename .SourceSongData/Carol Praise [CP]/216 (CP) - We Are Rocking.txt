"We are rocking"

1  We are rocking on our camels along the desert way,

following a Bethlehem star,

got to keep going, day and night,

following a Bethlehem star.

Well, the journey may be long and weary,

and a camel’s back is kind of rough,

but we’ve got to keep on moving now to keep it in sight

and follow the Bethlehem star. O come on

Follow, follow the Bethlehem star,

follow, follow the Bethlehem star,

you can follow wherever you are, come on and

follow, follow the Bethlehem star;

young and old it doesn’t really matter at all, you can

follow the Bethlehem star.

2  Well, the days are long and the nights are cold,

we’re gonna see the Bethlehem king,

the eastern star is shining bright,

gonna see the Bethlehem king;

and we’ll give him all our precious gifts

of gold and frankincense and myrrh.

But you can give to Jesus now whatever you’ve got –

so give your heart to the Bethlehem king. Then you can

Follow, follow the Bethlehem king,

follow, follow the Bethlehem king,

you can follow wherever you are, come on and

follow, follow the Bethlehem king;

young and old it doesn’t really matter at all, you can

follow the Bethlehem king.

Follow the Bethlehem king!
