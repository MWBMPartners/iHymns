"Hurry the Lord is near"

Chorus:
Alleluia! Hurry, the Lord is near.

Alleluia, alleluia!

Hurry, the Lord is near.

1  Sound the trumpet, the Lord is near;

hurry, the Lord is near;

see, he comes to save us all.

Hurry, the Lord is near!

Alleluia! Hurry...

2  Earth has longed for his approach:

hurry, the Lord is near;

straighten the road, smooth the path.

Hurry, the Lord is near!

Alleluia! Hurry...

3  Go out to meet him, shout his name:

hurry, the Lord is near;

his mighty kingdom shall never end.

Hurry, the Lord is near!

Alleluia! Hurry...

4  He is the mighty One, he is the Word;

hurry, the Lord is near;

God everlasting, prince of peace.

Hurry, the Lord is near!

Alleluia! Hurry...


Words by Patrick Lee

Music by Ernest Sands

Music arranged by Paul Inwood
