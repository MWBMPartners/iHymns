"Unto us a child is born"

1  People walking in the dark,

people living in a land of shadows –

now their darkness is dispelled,

for a light has dawned.

2  Unto us a child is born,

unto us a son is given,

and the government is his,

and he is called:

Wonderful Counsellor, Mighty God,

Everlasting Father, Prince of peace;

Wonderful...

3  He will reign for ever,

his peace will never end;

he will bring us joy,

will bring us life, our freedom defend.


Words and music by Mark Cheesman
