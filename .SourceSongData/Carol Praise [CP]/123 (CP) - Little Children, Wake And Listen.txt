"Little children, wake and listen"

1  Little children, wake and listen!

songs are filling all the earth;

while the stars in heaven glisten,

hear the news of Jesus’ birth.

Long ago, to lonely meadows

angels brought the message down;

still each year through midnight shadows

it is heard in every town.

2  Shepherds hurry to the stable

by the inn at Bethlehem,

run as fast as they are able

to the baby born for them.

People find the news amazing

as on that first Christmas morn:

let us join the shepherds praising

God, for Christ the king is born!
