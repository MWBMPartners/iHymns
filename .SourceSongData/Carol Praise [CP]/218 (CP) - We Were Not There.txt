"We were not there"

1  We were not there in Bethlehem

with shepherds in the night;

we could not see in starry sky

the dawn of heaven’s light –

and yet it shone for us.

2  We were not there in Bethlehem

where Mary laid her son;

we could not see in manger bed

his life on earth begun –

and yet he lived for us.


Words by Paul Wigmore
