"Rejoice with heart and voice"

Chorus:
Rejoice with heart and voice!

now is our Saviour

of the virgin Mary born –

so rejoice!

1  At this time our God fulfils

all our expectation:

let us offer hearts and wills

in rededication.

Rejoice...

2  God of God when time began,

Lord of all creation:

we revere the Son of Man

at his incarnation.

Rejoice...

3  Alleluia! Let us sing

hymns of adoration,

blessing Christ our worthy king

in this celebration!

Rejoice...


Words by Michael Perry

Music arranged by John Barnard
