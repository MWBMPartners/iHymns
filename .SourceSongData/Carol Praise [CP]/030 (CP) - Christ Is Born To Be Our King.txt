"Christ is born to be our king"

1  Christ is born to be our king –

listen, as the angels sing,

to the heavens echoing,

‘Glory be to God on high!’

2  Shepherds in the fields at night

hear the tidings, see the light,

find the child, in praise unite:

‘Glory be to God on high!’

3  Christians down the ages tell

Christ can break the powers of hell,

so that we may sing as well,

‘Glory be to God on high!’


Words by Michael Perry
