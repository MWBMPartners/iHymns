"To people of goodwill"

1  To people of goodwill,

be peace on earth and gladness!

Let joyful carols fill

the world where once was sadness.

So let us on this morn

lift up our hearts to heaven:

to us a Child is born,

to us a Son is given:

O praise him, O praise him, O praise him!

2  Through pain of sin’s distress,

all worldly pride unheeding,

in paths of righteousness

a little child is leading:

So let us...

3  All you that burdens bear,

come, take his yoke upon you:

your work he comes to share,

and lays his light load on you:

So let us...

4  O holy, humble birth –

now sing the joyful story!

Good will, good will on earth,

and in the highest glory!

So let us...
