"When the angel came to Mary"

1  When the angel came to Mary

he said, ‘Be at peace,

for the Lord God shall be with you,

his love will not cease.’

And Mary bore Jesus Christ,

our saviour for to be;

and the first and the last

and the greatest is he, is he, is he;

and the first and the last

and the greatest is he.

2  When the angel came to Mary

he said, ‘Do not fear,

for his power shall be upon you,

a child you will bear.’

And Mary bore Jesus Christ...

3  When the angel came to Mary

he said, ‘Hear his name,

for his title shall be Jesus

of kingly acclaim.’

And Mary bore Jesus Christ...

4  When the angel came to Mary,

she said, ‘Be it so;

for the Lord God is my master,

his will I must do.’

And Mary bore Jesus Christ...


Words by Michael Perry
