"Behold his love"

Behold his love!

I stand amazed and marvel

at the God of grace;

that the Alpha and Omega,

the Beginning and the End,

the Creator of the universe

on whom all life depends,

should be clothed in frail humanity

and suffer in my place.

Behold his love

and worship him, the God of grace.
