"Ring out the bells"

Chorus:
Ring out the bells –

the joyful news is breaking;

ring out the bells

for Jesus Christ is born!

1  Angels in wonder

sing of his glory;

shepherds returning

tell us the story.

Ring out...

2  Let all creation

worship before him;

earth bring him homage,

heaven adore him!

Ring out...

3  Prophets have spoken –

hark to their warning:

shadows are passing,

soon comes the morning!

Ring out...


Words by Michael Perry
