"Light shining in the darkness"

1  Light shining in the darkness,

light that is the world’s true light;

light from the beginning,

now as then, still shining bright.

People rejoice! the night is gone,

God’s redeeming work begun

in the birth of his Son.

2  Love came to dwell among us,

love made flesh at Bethlehem,

love from the beginning,

our divine Creator’s theme.

People rejoice...

3  Life full of truth and beauty,

life as it was meant to be,

life from the beginning,

and into eternity.

People rejoice...


Words by Anne Johnson
