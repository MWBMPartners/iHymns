"Still, still, still"

1  Still, still, still,

the baby lies asleep:

yet far away are herald voices –

heaven sings and earth rejoices!

Still, still, still,

the baby lies asleep.

2  Love, love, love,

no greater love than his;

while ‘Christ the Lord’ the angels name him,

we with fervent hearts acclaim him.

Love, love, love,

no greater love than his!


Words by Paul Wigmore

Music arranged by John Barnard
