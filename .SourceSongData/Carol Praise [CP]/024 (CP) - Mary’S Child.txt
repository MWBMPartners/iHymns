"Mary’s child"

1  Born in the night,

Mary’s child,

a long way from your home;

coming in need,

Mary’s child,

born in a borrowed room:

2  Clear shining light,

Mary’s child,

your face lights up our way:

light of the world,

Mary’s child,

dawn on our darkened day.

3  Truth of our life,

Mary’s child,

you tell us God is good:

prove it is true,

Mary’s child,

go to your cross of wood.

4  Hope of the world,

Mary’s child,

you’re coming soon to reign:

king of the earth,

Mary’s child,

walk in our streets again,

walk in our streets again.


Words and music by Geoffrey Ainger

Music arranged by Norman Warren
