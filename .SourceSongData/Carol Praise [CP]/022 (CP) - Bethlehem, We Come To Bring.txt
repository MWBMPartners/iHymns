"Bethlehem, we come to bring"

1  Bethlehem, we come to bring

merry dances for your king!

Step by step and drum by drum,

pat-a-pat-a-pan,

pat-a-pat-a-pan;

step by step and drum by drum

stepping bravely, here we come!

2  Mary, may we come to sing

merry songs to Christ our king?

Step by step and drum by drum,

pat-a-pat-a-pan,

pat-a-pat-a-pan;

step by step and drum by drum

stepping softly, here we come!

3  Christian people, now we say

merry Christmas on this day!

Step by step and drum by drum,

pat-a-pat-a-pan,

pat-a-pat-a-pan;

step by step and drum by drum

stepping gladly, here we come!


Words after by B de La Monnoye
