"We believe this is Jesus"

Chorus:
We believe this is Jesus –

come and see, come and see;

we believe this is Jesus –

come and see, come and see.

1  The light of God lights up his face –

come and see, come and see;

he offers us redeeming grace –

come and see, come and see.

We believe...

2  The love of God shines in his eyes –

come and see, come and see;

it tells of glory in the skies –

come and see, come and see.

We believe...

3  Did you ever see such love before –

come and see, come and see?

Go in peace and sin no more –

come and see, come and see!

We believe...
