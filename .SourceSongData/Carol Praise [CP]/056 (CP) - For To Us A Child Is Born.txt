"For to us a child is born"

Chorus:
For to us a child is born,

and to us a son is given,

and the government will be upon his shoulder;

of the increase of his rule there will be no end –

he will reign in righteousness and peace.

He’ll be called Wonderful,

Everlasting Father,

Counsellor, Prince of peace,

the Mighty God.
