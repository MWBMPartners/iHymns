"Come now, O Prince of peace"

1  Come now, O Prince of peace,

make us one body,

come, O Lord Jesus,

reconcile your people.

2  Come now, O God of love,

make us one body,

come, O Lord Jesus,

reconcile your people.

3  Come now and set us free,

O God, our Saviour,

come, O Lord Jesus,

reconcile all nations.

4  Come, Hope of unity,

make us one body,

come, O Lord Jesus,

reconcile all nations.


Words by Geonyong Lee

Words paraphrased by by Marion Pope
