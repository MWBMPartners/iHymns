# ⭐ Favorites

> Save and manage your favorite songs for quick access.

---

## Adding a Song to Favorites

1. Open any song's detail view
2. Tap the **star icon** (☆) next to the song title
3. The star fills in (★) to confirm the song has been saved

## Viewing Your Favorites

1. Tap the **Favorites** tab in the main navigation
2. Your saved songs are listed, sorted by most recently added
3. Tap any song to view its lyrics

## Removing a Song from Favorites

1. Open the song's detail view
2. Tap the **filled star icon** (★) to remove it
3. Alternatively, swipe left on a song in the Favorites list to remove it

---

## Notes

- Favorites are stored **locally on your device**
- On the Web PWA, favorites are saved in your browser's local storage
- Favorites do **not sync** between devices (this is planned for a future update)
- Clearing your browser data will remove web favorites
