# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

> Answers to common questions about iHymns.

---

## General

### What is iHymns?

iHymns is a multiplatform Christian lyrics application that provides searchable hymn and worship song lyrics from multiple songbooks. It's designed to enhance worship by making it easy to find and display song lyrics.

### Is iHymns free?

The web version at [iHymns.app](https://ihymns.app) is free to use. Native app availability and pricing may vary by platform.

### What songbooks are included?

iHymns currently includes five songbooks:

- The Church Hymnal (702 songs)
- Seventh-day Adventist Hymnal (695 songs)
- Mission Praise (3,517 songs)
- Junior Praise (1,787 songs)
- Carol Praise (714 songs)

### Can I request a new songbook?

Yes! Please [open a feature request](https://github.com/MWBMPartners/iHymns/issues) on GitHub.

---

## Features

### Can I use iHymns offline?

Yes. The web PWA caches all song data after your first visit. Native apps also work fully offline.

### Does iHymns have audio?

Songs from Mission Praise, Junior Praise, and Carol Praise include MIDI audio files. Audio playback is available where these files exist.

### Can I print song lyrics?

Yes. The web version includes a print-friendly stylesheet. Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P / Cmd+P) when viewing a song.

### Can I share songs?

On Apple devices, use the Share Sheet to share song lyrics via Messages, Email, AirDrop, and more. On the web, you can share the direct URL to any song.

---

## Technical

### What browsers are supported?

Any modern browser that supports ES2024+ JavaScript. We recommend Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.

### What Apple devices are supported?

- iPhone running iOS 17.0+
- iPad running iPadOS 17.0+
- Apple TV running tvOS 17.0+

### What Android devices are supported?

Android 8.0 (API 26) or later.

### Is my data private?

iHymns does not collect any personal data. Favorites and preferences are stored locally on your device only.
