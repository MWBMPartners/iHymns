# 🔍 Searching Songs

> How to find the hymn you're looking for, fast.

---

## Search Methods

### By Title

Type the song title (or part of it) into the search bar. Fuzzy matching means you don't need to spell it perfectly.

**Example**: Typing "amzing grace" will still find "Amazing Grace"

### By Lyrics

Search for a line or phrase from the song. This searches across all verses, choruses, and refrains.

**Example**: Typing "how sweet the sound" will find "Amazing Grace"

### By Song Number

Type the song number to find it directly within a songbook.

**Example**: Typing "CH 003" will find song #3 in The Church Hymnal

### By Songbook

Filter results by songbook using the songbook dropdown or by including the abbreviation in your search.

**Songbook abbreviations**:

- **CH** — The Church Hymnal
- **SDAH** — Seventh-day Adventist Hymnal
- **MP** — Mission Praise
- **JP** — Junior Praise
- **CP** — Carol Praise

### By Writer/Composer

Search by the writer or composer's name to find all their songs.

**Example**: Typing "Charles Wesley" will find all songs by Charles Wesley

---

## Search Tips

- Searches are **case-insensitive** — "jesus" and "Jesus" give the same results
- Use **fewer words** for broader results, more words to narrow down
- The search looks across **all fields** simultaneously (title, lyrics, number, writer)
- Results are ranked by **relevance** — the best match appears first
