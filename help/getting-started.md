# 🚀 Getting Started with iHymns

> How to install and start using iHymns on your device.

---

## 🌐 Web (Browser)

1. Visit [iHymns.app](https://ihymns.app) in any modern browser
2. Browse songbooks or use the search bar to find a hymn
3. **Install as an app**: When prompted, click "Install" to add iHymns to your home screen for offline access

### Supported Browsers

- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Safari (iOS/macOS)

---

## 🍎 Apple (iOS / iPadOS / tvOS)

1. Download **iHymns** from the App Store
2. Open the app and browse or search for hymns
3. Add songs to your Favorites for quick access

### System Requirements

- iOS 17.0 or later
- iPadOS 17.0 or later
- tvOS 17.0 or later

---

## 🤖 Android

1. Download **iHymns** from the Google Play Store
2. Open the app and start exploring hymns

### System Requirements

- Android 8.0 (API 26) or later

---

## 🔍 Quick Tips

- Use the **search bar** to find songs by title, lyrics, songbook, or song number
- Tap the **star icon** to save a song to your Favorites
- Use **dark mode** for comfortable reading in low light
